# Plating Ritual: Cardamom Candle + Perfume

## Components

- Cardamom Oil

- Cardamom–Saffron Candle

- Walnut Wick

- Cardamom Perfume (pods blended in water, strained)

## Process

1. Keep plates and candles at −30 °C.

2. Brush plate with Cardamom Oil.

3. Set candle on plate.

4. Spray Cardamom Perfume.

5. Insert Walnut Wick.

6. Light wick just before the plate leaves the pass.

## Parameters

- **Temperature**: −30 °C (plates and candles)

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: A multi-sensory aromatic experience for the guest.
