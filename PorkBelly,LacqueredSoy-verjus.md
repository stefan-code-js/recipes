# Pork Belly, Lacquered Soy-Verjus

## Prep & Cook

- **Cure**: 2% salt, 1% sugar, spices, 24 h; rinse.

- **Cook**: Confit in fat at **120 °C for 3 h**; press overnight; portion.

## Glaze & Finish

- **Glaze**: Soy + verjus + honey reduced to **104 °C**; torch to shine.

## Parameters

- **Cure Salt/Sugar**: 2% / 1%

- **Confit Temp**: 120 °C

- **Confit Time**: 3 h

- **Glaze Temp**: 104 °C

## Plating Cues

- **Finish**: Lacquered shine.
