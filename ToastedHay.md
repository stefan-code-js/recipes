# Toasted Hay

## Ingredients

- 100 g hay

## Process

1. Toast hay in deep pan, with a perforated pan on top.

2. Cook at 200 °C fan for 45–60 min.

3. Store airtight when nutty.

## Parameters

- **Temperature**: 200 °C (fan)

- **Time**: 45–60 min

## Sensory Cues

- **Stop Cue**: When nutty.

- **Result**: Aromatic hay for infusions or smoking.
