# Mushroom Dashi

## Technique

Toast dried mushrooms and kombu for 10 min at 140 °C → steep at 80 °C for 45 min → remove kombu at 60 min to avoid bitterness → reduce 20–30%.

## Parameters

- **Toast Temp**: 140 °C

- **Toast Time**: 10 min

- **Steep Temp**: 80 °C

- **Steep Time**: 45 min (total steep), remove kombu at 60 min.

- **Reduction**: 20–30%
