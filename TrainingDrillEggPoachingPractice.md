# Training Drill: Egg Poaching Practice

## Goal

Build a poach time chart by egg size and water temperature.

## Practice

1. Poach 10 eggs, each 15 seconds apart from 2 min 30 s to 4 min.

2. Slice all, photograph the white and yolk textures.

3. Build your poach time chart by egg size and water temp.
