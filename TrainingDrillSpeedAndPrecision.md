# Training Drill: Speed and Precision

## Goal

Achieve consistency under pressure.

## Practice

1. Time yourself making:

- 1 vinaigrette

- 1 purée

- 1 pan sauce reduction

- 1 portion plating

1. Record: start to finish.

2. Repeat weekly; focus on cleaner setup and zero wasted motion.

3. **Benchmark**: A professional line cook should have **≤ 10% time variance** between runs.
