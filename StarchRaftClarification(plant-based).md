# Starch Raft Clarification (Plant-Based)

## Technique

Blend potato peels + mirepoix → bring to a simmer → hold 30 min → **do not boil** → skim and filter.

## Parameters

- **Hold Time**: 30 min

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: Clarified stock/jus.
