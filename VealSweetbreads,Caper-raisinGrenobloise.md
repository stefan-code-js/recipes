# Veal Sweetbreads, Caper-Raisin Grenobloise

## Prep & Cook

- **Prep**: Milk soak 2 h; blanch 2 min; press cold 1 h; peel membranes.

- **Cook**: Roast in brown butter.

- **Finish**: Glaze with caper-raisin–lemon brown “beurre” (Grenobloise).

## Parameters

- **Soak Time**: 2 h

- **Blanch Time**: 2 min

- **Press Time**: 1 h

## Plating Cues

- **Texture**: Exterior nutty, center custard.
