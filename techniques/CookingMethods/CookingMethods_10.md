# Mushroom Royale, Truffle Vinaigrette, Herb Salad

## Components

- Silken mushroom custard

- Warm truffle vinaigrette

- Micro herb salad

## Method

- Steam custard at 85 °C to just set.

- Spoon warm vinaigrette.

- Crown with micro herbs.

## Parameters

- **Steam Temp**: 85 °C (custard)

- **Service Temp**: Warm (vinaigrette)

## Plating Cues

- **Texture**: Spoon-tremble center (custard).

- **Aroma**: Aromatic volatility (truffle).

## Creative Pairing

A delicate, silken mushroom custard served warm to maximize the truffle's volatile aroma, contrasted by the fresh, cool herb salad.
