# Training Drill: Stock and Jus Quality

## Goal

Produce clean, gelatin-rich base with no bitterness.

## Practice: Roast Tone

1. Roast bones at 190 Â°C to different browns: light (30 min), medium (60), dark (90).

2. Make three small stocks from each.

3. Taste for clarity and bitterness.

4. Pick the color tone your restaurant should use as standard.

## Practice: Gel Strength

1. Chill the three stocks.

2. Compare gel strength to gauge collagen extraction.
