# Smoked Mushroom Garum Butter

## Ingredients

- 200 mL plant garum (mushroom-yeast base)

- 100 g cultured vegan butter

- smoked salt, to taste

## Process

1. Reduce garum to 50 mL.

2. Whisk into softened butter with smoked salt.

3. Chill and shape.

## Parameters

- **Reduction**: Garum reduced to 50 mL (¼ volume)

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: umami concentrate; finish sauces, or baste charred brassicas.

## Creative Application

Use the chilled butter to baste charred brassicas or finish a rich, dark sauce for a concentrated umami boost.
