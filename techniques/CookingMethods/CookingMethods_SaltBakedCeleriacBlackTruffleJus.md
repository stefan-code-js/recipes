# Salt-Baked Celeriac, Black Truffle Jus

## Components

- Whole celeriac baked in salt crust

- Black truffle–mushroom jus

- Toasted hazelnut oil

- Celery leaf salad

## Method

- Bake celeriac at 190 °C for 2–2.5 h.

- Reduce mushroom dashi with truffle trim to nappé consistency.

- Carve tableside.

## Parameters

- **Bake Temp**: 190 °C

- **Bake Time**: 2–2.5 h

- **Jus Finish**: Reduce to nappé.

## Plating Cues

- **Texture**: Interior should carve like roast.

- **Viscosity**: Jus coats spoon.

- **Finish**: Finish with hazelnut oil drops.

## Creative Pairing

The tableside carving ritual emphasizes the celeriac's transformation, with the rich, reduced truffle jus providing a deep, earthy counterpoint to the vegetable's sweetness.
