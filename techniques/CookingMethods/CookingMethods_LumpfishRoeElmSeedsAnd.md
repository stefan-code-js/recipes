## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Lumpfish Roe, Elm Seeds, and 
Grilled Ramsons 
Lightly cooked and raw egg yolks are cured in beef garum and whisked 
with smoked butter whey and more beef garum to make a sauce

2. Scallops are pureed and then dried until caramelized and crisp like 
a chip

3. The scallop chips are processed into a powder, which is blended 
with oil and beeswax to make scallop fudge

4. Lumpfish roe is gently folded into the egg yolk sauce and spooned 
into the serving dish

5. Roasted kelp salt is folded into the middle of the 
roe and egg yolk sauce

6. A rose and yeast sauce is spooned around the 
roe, and rose oil and elm seeds are sprinkled on top

7. Ramsons are brushed with smoked butter and grilled over hot 
charcoal until caramelized and slightly crisp but juicy

8. The grilled 
ramsons are dressed with warm scallop fudge, smoked butter, 
horseradish juice, and salt and served alongside the dressed lumpfish roe
