# Rose Kombucha Glaze

## Ingredients

- 500 mL rose-petal kombucha

- 40 g glucose or honey

## Process

1. Reduce kombucha over medium heat to a thin syrup (⅓ volume).

2. Add glucose; cool to room temp.

## Parameters

- **Reduction**: ⅓ volume

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: glossy, tart-floral glaze for roasted root vegetables or cold fruit carpaccio.

## Creative Application

Brush the glaze onto roasted root vegetables during the last few minutes of cooking to create a glossy, tart-floral crust.
