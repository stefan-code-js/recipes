# Training Drill: Finishing and Resting (Carryover Cooking)

## Goal

Learn carryover cooking and set new pull temperatures.

## Practice

1. Roast a 500 g steak to 50 째C, 55 째C, 60 째C.

2. Remove and rest 5, 10, 15 min.

3. Recheck temp after rest.

4. **Observation**: Note internal rise (carryover), usually +3 to +6 째C.

5. **Action**: Set new pull temps for your service line based on this data.
