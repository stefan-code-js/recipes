Minerals
Minerals, like vitamins, are consumed in very small quantities and are essential for regulating  
certain body processes. Minerals that must be consumed in relatively large amounts—more than 
100 milligrams daily—are called major minerals. these include calcium, chloride, magnesium, 
phosphorus, sulfur, sodium, and potassium. Minerals that must be present in smaller amounts
