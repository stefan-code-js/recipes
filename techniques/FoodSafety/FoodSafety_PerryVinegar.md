# Perry Vinegar

## Ingredients

- 1 L pear cider (\~6–8 % ABV)

- vinegar mother

## Process

1. Pasteurize cider; cool.

2. Add mother.

3. Aerate 2–3 weeks.

## Parameters

- **Temperature**: 70 °C (pasteurization), 30 °C (aeration)

- **Time**: 2–3 weeks (aeration)

- **Acidity**: \~5 % (target)

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: elegant floral vinegar for pickles or gastriques.

- **Stop Cue**: When \~5 % acidity is reached.

## Creative Application

Use the floral vinegar to make a gastrique that balances the richness of pork belly or a savory fruit tart.
