4. To do the test, poke the rising loaf with a floured finger, making an

5. If it springs back immediately, the loaf needs more
proofing time

6. If the indentation springs back slowly and incompletely, the loaf is fully
proofed and ready to bake

7. If the indentation doesn’t spring back at all, the loaf is
overproofed

8. You’ve waited too long, and the loaf may collapse a bit when you remove
