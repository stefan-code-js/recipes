## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. DAYS-OLD BREAD
267Once the livers have cooled, add the remaining 4 tablespoons butter to the food 
processor and process to a thick puree

2. Add the salt and the remaining ¼ cup 
cognac and process again

3. Taste and add more salt if needed

4. Pour the liver puree 
into ramekins or into a suitably sized loaf or pâté pan

5. To make the cognac butter, place the butter in a small bowl

6. In a small sauce -
pan, heat the cognac until it is hot to the touch

7. Add it to the butter along with 
the salt

8. Stir the butter until it has a liquid consistency and then pour it evenly 
over the pâté

9. Cover and refrigerate until the cognac butter has set

10. Serve cool 
or at room temperature with toast.
