# Miso & Peaso: Thesis & Baseline

**Thesis**: Legume-based miso variants ("peaso") unlock savory pastes in months, not years, using koji.

**Baseline Playbook (Peaso)**:

1. Cook and grind legumes.

2. Mix with koji + **\~4% salt**; adjust with 4% brine if dry.

3. Pack tight, weight, and seal.

4. Ferment at room temp (**22–30 °C**) for **≥3 months**.

**Safety Rail**: Use **\~4% salt** (8–10% to slow); ferment at **22–30 °C** for a minimum of **3 months**.
