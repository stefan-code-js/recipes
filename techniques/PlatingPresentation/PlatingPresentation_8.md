# Training Drill: Vegetable Mastery (Texture and Color)

## Goal

Control vegetable texture and color.

## Practice: Blanching Control

1. Cook 100 g spinach at 45, 60, 75, 85, and 95 °C.

2. Compare color retention and bite.

3. **Perfect Green**: Record “perfect green” time (usually 60–75 s at 85–90 °C, in 2% salted water).

## Practice: Caramelization Curve

1. Dice carrots uniformly.

2. Roast trays at 150, 170, 190, 210 °C for 10, 20, 30 min.

3. Taste sugars, bitterness, and texture.

## Practice: Purée Consistency

1. Blend same vegetable purée with 10%, 20%, 30% fat.

2. Compare mouthfeel and plate flow.

3. Note optimum fat % for each root.
