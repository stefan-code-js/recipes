## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Combine the mixed greens and Roquefort in a medium-sized bowl. Toss with as much of the almond vinaigrette as needed to lightly coat the greens. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

2. To serve, divide the salad into 6 equal portions and place each on a decorative fork. Lay a fork on a small plate and drizzle a little vinaigrette next to the fork. Repeat to make 5 more servings.
