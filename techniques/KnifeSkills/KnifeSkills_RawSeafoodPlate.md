## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Raw Seafood Plate 
Mussels are steamed in white wine and the broth is lightly set with 
gelatin, frozen, and then strained as it thaws to clarify

2. Sugar kelp is 
cooked in the ice-clarified mussel broth until tender, then torn into 
squares

3. Slices of live Norwegian scallop and its roe are arranged on a serving 
plate and topped with a dot of black currant wood fudge

4. Fresh Skagen 
shrimp are nestled among the scallop slices and topped ,"7ith a small 
pile of horseradish paste

5. Pieces of sugar kelp are tucked between the 
seafood, ice-clarified mussel broth is spooned around the seafood, and 
a few drops of koji and horseradish oils finish the dish
