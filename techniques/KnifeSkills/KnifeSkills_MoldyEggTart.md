## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Moldy Egg Tart 
A beef garum-cured egg yolk is dusted with rice flour, inoculated with 
Aspergillus oryzae spores, and fermented for two days until the koji 
mold covers the yolk, then trimmed and gently flattened

2. A seaweed dough is rolled thin and baked into tart shells, ,,vhich are 
then lined with wedge-shaped pieces of nasturtium leaf

3. The leaves are 
dotted with horseradish oil and spread with koji truffle fudge

4. Preserved 
truffle slices are placed on the koji truffle fudge

5. The truffle slices are 
then covered with a layer of truffle puree

6. The moldy egg yolk is placed 
on the truffle puree and more puree is piped around it

7. The ring of 
puree is sprinkled with finely chopped preserved truffle and topped 
with individual grains of koji
