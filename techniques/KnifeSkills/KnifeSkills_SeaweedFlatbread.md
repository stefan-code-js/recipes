## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Seaweed Flatbread 
Cod roe is lightly salted and cold-smoked over hay, then blended with 
lemon juice and emulsified with oil and brown butter

2. More roe is 
folded into the emulsion

3. Irish moss is cooked with mushroom-kelp broth

4. Lightly cooked ovals of sugar kelp are coated in tempura batter, 
deep-fried, and showered with lemon verbena, oregano, and oxalis

5. Dots of parsley paste, sliced salted green gooseberries, a piping of cod 
roe emulsion, and a dusting of samphire and plankton powder dress the 
crisp as well

6. The crisps are arranged on a napkin with Mexican oregano 
leaves, marinated sea lettuce, Irish moss, and Japanese quince sliced 
into fans
