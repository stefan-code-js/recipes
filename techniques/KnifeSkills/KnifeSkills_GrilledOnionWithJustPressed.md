# Grilled Onion with Just-Pressed

**Category:** Knife Skills  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

---

## Overview

Grilled Onion with Just-Pressed 
Walnut Oil 
Onions with their green tops intact are deeply charred in white-hot 
coals and then cooked over direct heat until tender. The heart (inner 
layers) of the onion is scooped out and sliced, and the slices dressed 
with red pepper tamari, chives, and smoked salt. The onion heart is 
reassembled and slipped �ack into the outer layers of the onion. The 
interior is seasoned with just-pressed walnut oil and the onion is closed 
up and served.
