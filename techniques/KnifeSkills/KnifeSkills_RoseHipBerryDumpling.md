## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Rose Hip Berry Dumpling 
A rose hip is seeded from the bottom and then peeled, leaving the 
stem end intact

2. The seeded rose hip is brushed with rose oil and lightly 
dried

3. The top of the semi-dried rose hip is cut off and the cut edge is 
piped with Japanese quince paste and pinched to seal, creating a hollow 
dumpling

4. The interior is filled with diced Japanese quince, a red currant, and 
a drop of black currant wood oil

5. The mirabelle gel is added, then the 
Japanese quince paste, and finally the rose hip-red currant paste; the 
stuffed rose hip dumpling is then sealed

6. The dumpling is finished with 
rose oil, morita chile oil, and cut coriander seeds
