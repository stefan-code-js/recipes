## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Berry Sauce  
Grilled Lemon Thyme Paste  
Picked lemon thyme flowers  
Open the husk, and spoon in the 8 marinated berries and a teaspoon of walnut fudge, and then 
place the 5 pieces of cut corn in over the top, keeping it as whole looking as possible

2. Leaving the bundle open, grill it husk -side do wn on a medium temperature barbecue to warm it for a 
few minutes

3. Once warm, remove the corn from the grill, and season it with a bit more walnutfudge, a couple drops of the red pepper tamari, the grilled lemon thyme paste, and a bit of salt.Garnish with  lemon thyme flowers and serve immediately on a napkin -lined plate
