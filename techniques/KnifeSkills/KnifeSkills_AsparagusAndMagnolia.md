## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Asparagus and Magnolia 
White asparagus spears are trimmed and peeled, then cooked in 
asparagus juice (made from the trimmings) seasoned with lemon thyme 
and verbena until they yield a juicy bite

2. The spears are then cooled in a 
similar broth

3. The spears are pierced through the cut end with an apple blossom 
skewer, then seasoned with salt and balsamic vinegar

4. Fresh strawberry 
slices are shingled on top, leaving the asparagus tip unseasoned and 
uncovered

5. The strawberry slices are seasoned with a few small drops of 
elderflower oil, then topped with a thin piping of strawberry paste, 
made from fresh strawberries caramelized in koji oil and then pureed

6. A brined magnolia leaf is spritzed with ginger juice, then gently pressed 
onto the paste

7. To finish, three or four brined magnolia pistils are placed 
on the tip of the white asparagus
