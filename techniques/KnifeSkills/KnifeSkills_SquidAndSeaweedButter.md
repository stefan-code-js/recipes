## Overview

Squid and Seaweed Butter 
Thinly sliced squares of squid are brushed with spruce wood oil and 
briefly cooked. 
The squid trimmings are grilled with smoked koji oil over charcoal, 
blended with dashi, and cooked; the liquid is then frozen and strained 
as it thaws to clarify. This clarified grilled squid broth is combined with 
Nordic shoyu, chicken wing garum, peaso water reduction, berry spice, 
and roasted kelp salt, then blended with melted butter to make squid 
and seaweed sauce.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Squid and Seaweed Butter 
Thinly sliced squares of squid are brushed with spruce wood oil and 
briefly cooked

2. The squid trimmings are grilled with smoked koji oil over charcoal, 
blended with dashi, and cooked; the liquid is then frozen and strained 
as it thaws to clarify

3. This clarified grilled squid broth is combined with 
Nordic shoyu, chicken wing garum, peaso water reduction, berry spice, 
and roasted kelp salt, then blended with melted butter to make squid 
and seaweed sauce

4. The squid squares are folded into a diamond shape, arranged on a 
black currant wood mat, and briefly heated

5. Each diamond is dolloped 
with horseradish and sea buckthorn paste and then sauced, tableside, 
with squid and seaweed sauce
