## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Radish and Nasturtium Butterfly 
A dough is made from flour, kelp in several forms, and butter, then 
rolled thin, cut into a butterfly shape, and baked into a tart shell

2. Fresh coriander leaves are flash frozen, ground, and then mixed 
with yeast broth reduction to make a paste

3. Radishes are cooked in an 
infused liquid, then sliced thin and cut into half-moons

4. The butterfly pastry is piped with vegetable fudge and coriander 
paste

5. The bottom wing sections are covered with radish slices folded in 
half and shingled onto the pastry in a ruffled pattern

6. The upper wing 
sections are layered with overlapping nasturtium and sage blossom 
petals

7. The butterfly's body and antennae are showered with grated 
horseradish and the whole dish is seasoned with salt and drops of 
horseradish juice and horseradish oil
