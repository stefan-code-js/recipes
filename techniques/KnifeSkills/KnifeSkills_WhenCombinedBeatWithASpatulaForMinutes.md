## Overview

When combined, beat with a spatula for 10 minutes  
to make the mixture light and airy.

orange peel.  Fit the stand mixer with the whisk, mix the almond paste  
with the sugar, and then beat in the eggs, one at a time.
Spread out the hazelnuts on a baking sheet and roast
