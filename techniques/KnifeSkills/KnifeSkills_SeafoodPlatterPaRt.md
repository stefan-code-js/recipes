## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Seafood Platter, Part 3 
Venus clams are steamed in cucumber juice seasoned with coriander 
seed, dried angelica, and lemon thyme

2. Sea buckthorn, juniper berries, 
angelica, green coriander, pine shoots, and Douglas fir needles are 
muddled together with mirabelle plum juice to make a sauce for the 
clams

3. The trimmed steamed clams are placed upright in a clam shell 
on some purslane leaves, with more purslane in between the clams

4. The 
shell is filled with the Venus clam sauce

5. Carpet clams are cooked in clarified fennel juice, sliced into pieces, 
and arranged along the lip of a carpet clam shell

6. The center of the shell 
is filled with cooked mixed grains that have been folded with koji fudge

7. Oxalis leaves top the grains and a sorrel and clam sauce is piped into the 
shell

8. Razor clam grains are folded with some hazelnut fudge and chopped 
razor clam skirts and then spooned into an empty razor clam shell

9. The grains are dressed with a few dots of hazelnut marinade

10. Sliced 
razor clam flesh is alternately shingled with thin slices of salted fresh 
hazelnuts
