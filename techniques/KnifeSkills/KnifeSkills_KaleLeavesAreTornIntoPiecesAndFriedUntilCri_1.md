# Kale leaves are torn into pieces and fried until crisp. Baby Gem lettuce

**Category:** Knife Skills  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

---

## Overview

•King Crab Salad
Kale leaves are torn into pieces and fried until crisp. Baby Gem lettuce 
leaves are trimmed down almost to the ribs, which are cut into short 
pieces. 
Sunflower seeds are roasted in butter with Danish curry powder 
and other seasonings, then ground into a paste. Freeze-dried rhubarb is 
rehydrated in dashi, seasoned with berry spice, and caramelized in koji 
oil. The caramelized rhubarb is blended with the·sunflower seed paste 
and fried parsley to make king crab salad paste.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. •King Crab Salad
Kale leaves are torn into pieces and fried until crisp

2. Baby Gem lettuce 
leaves are trimmed down almost to the ribs, which are cut into short 
pieces

3. Sunflower seeds are roasted in butter with Danish curry powder 
and other seasonings, then ground into a paste

4. Freeze-dried rhubarb is 
rehydrated in dashi, seasoned with berry spice, and caramelized in koji 
oil

5. The caramelized rhubarb is blended with the·sunflower seed paste 
and fried parsley to make king crab salad paste

6. The lower joints of king crab legs are brushed with smoked butter 
and grilled briefly over hot charcoal

7. The meat is removed and piped 
with the king crab salad paste

8. The leaves and tender stems of a variety of fresh herbs are tossed 
with green coriander seed halves and dressed with pine vinegar and 
horseradish juice

9. The lettuce ribs are blanched in a koji emulsion, combined with the 
fried kale leaves and most of the crab, and dressed in hot melted spiced 
king crab butter

10. The dressed fresh herbs are added and everything 
is gently shaken to mix, then transferred to a serving dish in which a 
horn spoon has been placed
