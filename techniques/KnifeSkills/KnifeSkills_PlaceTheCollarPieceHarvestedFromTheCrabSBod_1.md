## Overview

barbecue and allow the pieces to slowly cook through for roughly 30 minutes over indirect  heat 
until the flesh seizes up and is warm to the touch when tested with a cake tester. Ensure that the collar is being glazed continuously with the collar glaze to keep it from drying out. It should

## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Place the collar piece harvested from the crab’s body on a middle rack floating above the 
barbecue and allow the pieces to slowly cook through for roughly 30 minutes over indirect  heat 
until the flesh seizes up and is warm to the touch when tested with a cake tester

2. Ensure that the collar is being glazed continuously with the collar glaze to keep it from drying out

3. It should 
be heavily coated with glaze the whole time

4. Just before it’s done cooking, transfer the collar to 
the bottom rack of the grill to kiss it with high heat just briefly, and the color the shell just slightly

5. Time the cooking of the crab collar to be finished at the same time the crab legs are called out

6. Begin cooking the legs with the 
thickest portion and knuckle facing inward so that the legs cook evenly, before moving the 
whole leg over the heat to carry it through

7. The cooking of the legs should take 6 to 8 min utes,
temping it with a cake tester until warm to the touch

8. Ensure that the crab collar is 
simultaneously hot and ready when the crab legs are called out

9. Proceed to remove the legs from the barbecue and transfer them to a cutting board
