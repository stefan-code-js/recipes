## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Mexican Oregano with 
Oyster Paste 
Fresh gigas oysters are pureed and emulsified with parsley leaves and 
parsley oil, and freshened with a bit of lemon

2. The oyster liquor is 
strained and reserved

3. Thick pieces of kombu are braised until just tender in a stock 
flavored with dried mushrooms, lacto-fermented cep water, 
lingonberries, and sugar

4. The kombu is cut into diamond shapes and 
dusted with a powder made from dried samphire, dried sea lettuce, and 
roasted kelp salt

5. A Mexican oregano leaf is brushed on one side with the oyster 
emulsion and sprayed on the other with reserved oyster liquor

6. Rocks 
are arranged on the serving plate and the oregano leaf is placed on the 
rocks, with a dusted kombu diamond placed on either side of the spine

7. A dot of oyster emulsion is piped into the center of a rosette of oyster 
leaf plant, which then receives a drop of horseradish juice, a spray of 
oyster liquor, and a few grains of salt

8. The rosette is then positioned 
to look as if it is growing from under the rocks with other wild beach 
plants, such as purslane, ice plant, and beach mustard
