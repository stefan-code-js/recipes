## Overview

## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Noble Fir Cone on the Cob 
Noble fir cones are prepared by picking out the woody scales, pine nuts, 
and seed wings that sit between the scales of the cone

2. The cones are cut 
into smaller lengths, then marinated

3. Forest skewers are fashioned from sharpened spruce or Douglas fir 
tips with flowering herbs tied on

4. Pickled rose petals and marinated lacto-fermented cherry halves 
are slid between several scales of the fir cone

5. The cones are basted 
with smoked butter and grilled over hot charcoal, and the marinated 
pine shoots and black currant shoots are tucked between a few more 
scales

6. The forest skewers are speared into the ends of the chunk of fir 
cone and spruce fudge is spread all over it

7. The fir cone is dusted with 
woodruff and Japanese quince za'atar and spritzed with woodruff 
distillate
