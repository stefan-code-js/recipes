## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Cod Roe Wattle 
Buckwheat-corn waffles are cooked in brown butter

2. Grapeseed oil and 
smoked butter are infused with Nordic seaweed spice and ancho chile to 
make confit oil

3. Cod roe is soaked in a spiced brine, lightly cured in a barley koji 
paste, and then gently cooked in the confit oil with juniper berries

4. The 
confited cod roe is sliced into thin rounds

5. The waffle is brushed with hazelnut oil and piped with veg fudge 
and smoked egg yolk paste

6. The cod roe slices are laid on top and 
dressed with hazelnut oil, morita chile oil, and koji-cep glaze

7. Pumpkin and sunflower seeds from savory granola along with lemon 
thyme leaves and black currant caper halves are arranged around the 
edges of the waffle
