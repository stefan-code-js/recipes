## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. 686 Chapter 22  salad dressings and salads

Peaches should be plump and firm, without bruises or blemishes

2. a void dark green fruits, which are 
immature and will not ripen well

3. a void fruits refrigerated before ripening, as they may be mealy

4. select freestone varieties of peaches

5. Clingstone varieties require too much labor (they are used 
primarily for canning)

6. let ripen at room temperature, then refrigerate

7. Peel peaches by blanching in boiling water 
10–20 seconds, until skin slips off easily, and cool in ice water

8. (nectarines do not need to be peeled.)
