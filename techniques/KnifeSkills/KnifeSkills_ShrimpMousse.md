## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Shrimp Mousse 
Raw skagen shrimp are blended with cream and rose oil and then folded 
with whipped cream to make a mousse

2. The mousse is aerated and 
frozen under vacuum, then sliced into rectangular portions

3. Dried mirabelle plum blossoms are steamed '"'ith oil and infused, 
and the oil is strained

4. A spoonful of this mirabelle blossom oil is added to the serving 
plate, along with roasted kelp salt, horseradish juice, and skinned 
toasted beechnuts

5. One side of the frozen aerated shrimp mousse 
receives a dusting of powdered lacto-fermented plum skin

6. The mousse 
is then placed atop the other elements, undusted-side up
