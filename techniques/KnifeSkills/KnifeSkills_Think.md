and starts telling me stories when I’m tired and want to stay focused on my work

2. Think, 
Rollie’s a great guy

3. Scale out amounts of flour and
water in accordance with formula sheets prepared the day before

4. Mix on first
speed until the water and flour are incorporated

5. 5:00 A.M.:
 Begin baking levain breads (Country Blonde, Country Brown, the big
boules

night slowly proofing in the retarder

6. For the next 20 minutes, load 144 regular-size
levain loaves into the oven
