# Quail eggs are soft-boiled and lightly smoked over hay.

**Category:** Knife Skills  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

---

## Overview

, Rose Hip Sausage with Quail Egg 
Quail eggs are soft-boiled and lightly smoked over hay. 
Reeves plums are simmered in spiced dashi until tender, then 
peeled. The plum flesh is cooked down with rose hips, spices, and 
dried rose oil and the mixture is finely chopped to make a paste. Green 
coriander seeds and fennel flowers are chopped and folded into the rose 
hip and plum paste. The mixture is rolled into a sausage shape, dried, 
and brushed with plum juice reduction and chicken wing garum.
