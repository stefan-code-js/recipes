## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Hipberry Marinated with 
Marigold and Pollen 
A rose hip is skinned, seeded, brushed with marigold oil, and dried 
until chewy

2. Japanese quince paste is gently folded with bee pollen and 
spooned into the rose hip, which is then garnished with green coriander 
seed halves

3. Japanese quince paste is piped onto a trimn1ed nasturtium leaf and 
a thin slice of salted Japanese quince is laid on top

4. The quince slice is 
brushed with marigold oil, and marigold petals are arranged around 
the edge in a flower pattern

5. The center of the nasturtium leaf is piped 
with more Japanese quince paste and covered with bee pollen

6. The 
nasturtium "marigold" is positioned on the stuffed rose hip, hiding the 
rose hip beneath

7. A drizzle of marigold oil encircles the stuffed hip berry 
to finish the dish
