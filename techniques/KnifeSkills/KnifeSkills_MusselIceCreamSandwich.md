## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Mussel Ice Cream Sandwich 
Conference pears are aged and caramelized under vacuum for two 
months, then dried until sticky

2. The black pears are blended with 
Conference pear puree and dried into a thin fruit leather, which is 
draped over mussel shells to shape

3. An ice cream is made with smoked cream and roasted kelp and 
beeswax caramel

4. Roasted beet juice is reduced with kelp, then mixed 
with freeze-dried black currants and green gooseberry water and set 
with pectin

5. Bilberry crisp ingredients are cooked and rolled thin, then mussel 
shell shapes are cut out and baked until dry

6. The fruit leather mussel shells are filled with the roasted kelp salt 
and beeswax ice cream and a small indentation is scooped out of the 
center of the ice cream

7. The beet reduction is piped into the center of 
the ice cream

8. A bilberry crisp is dusted with black currant and licorice powder, 
piped with salted licorice syrup, and seasoned with roasted kelp salt

9. The seasoned crisp is sandwiched between two ice cream -filled 
fruit leather mussel shell halves

10. The ice cream sandwich is served on 
a rock plate
