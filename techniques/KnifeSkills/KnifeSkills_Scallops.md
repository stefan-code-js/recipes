## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Sprinkle the scallops with salt and a little sugar, then 
place in the refrigerator for 24 hours to cure

2. Slice each scallop into 
3 and put 5 slices on each plate

3. Keep the plates in the refrigerator 
until serving

Wearing gloves, use strong scissors to cut carefully into the bottom 
of each sea urchin and work towards the sides

5. Cut all the way around 
the shell, taking care not to prick your fingers on the sharp spikes, 
and remove the bottom

6. Strain the juice and reserve it in the 
refrigerator

7. Rinse the insides of the sea urchins with cold water 
and remove the edible orange 'tongues' with a small teaspoon

8. Clean off any impurities and freeze the sea urchin 'tongues' in a 
Paco container
