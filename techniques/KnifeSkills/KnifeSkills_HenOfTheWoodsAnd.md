## Overview

Hen of the Woods and 
Madagascar Pepper 
Madagascar peppercorns are marinated in maitake oil, slowly smoked 
over birch,-vood, and then finely chopped. 
Fresh wild mushroom slices are basted with mushroom cooking 
glaze as they're being grilled over charcoal. The grilled mushroom slices 
are brushed with dashi reduction, seasoned with rose oil, pine vinegar, 
salt, and cold-infused kelp salt and finished with finely chopped smoked 
Madagascar peppercorns.
