## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. heat the oil over moderate heat in a saucepan.

2. add the curry paste and cook until aromatic. 1½ oz 45 mL red curry paste (see sidebar)

3. Stir in the water or stock and bring to a simmer.

4. add the coconut milk and nam pla. Bring to a simmer

5. Combine all ingredients in a food processor.

6. refrigerate and use as needed to make curry
