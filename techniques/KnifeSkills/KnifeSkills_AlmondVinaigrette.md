## Overview

## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Combine the two oils in a glass measuring cup with a spout.

2. Put the almonds and vinegar in a blender and puree until smooth and pastelike. With the motor running, slowly add the oils and blend until emulsified.

3. Transfer to a small glass or ceramic bowl and season to taste with salt and pepper. Use immediately or refrigerate the vinaigrette in a tightly lidded nonreactive container for up to 3 days. Whisk well before using.
