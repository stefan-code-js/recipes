## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Roast Waterfowl and Foraged Plate 
Roast Waterfowl 
To make the hazelnut feather, flatbread dough is rolled thin, cut into 
a feather shape, and baked

2. Truffles are blended with mushroom 
kelp broth, lacto-fermented cep water, and celery reduction and then 
emulsified with yeast oil and cep oil to create a thick, creamy puree

3. The 
feather is piped with soft cheese and layered with the truffle puree and 
sliced truffles

4. Vegetable fudge is piped on either side of the center quill, 
and finely julienned fresh hazelnuts are positioned to replicate the barbs 
of the feather

5. Fresh truffles are sliced, brushed with cep oil, and spritzed with 
lacto-fermented cep water and then layered with baby spinach and 
truffle puree to create a truffle millefeuille

6. A wild duck breast crown is cooked over charcoal until deeply 
caramelized, then finished with duck finishing glaze and sliced

7. The 
slices are spread with berry praline and sprinkled with chopped coldÂ­
smoked Madagascar peppercorns

8. The duck is plated and a warm truffle sauce is ladled over

9. Grape 
leaves are spritzed with pine vinegar and arranged on the plate with 
the duck, along with the hazelnut feather and the truffle millefeuille

10. A 
foraged plate is served alongside
