## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. TARTINE BREAD
264To make the beef patties, prepare a fire in a charcoal grill about 30 minutes 
before serving the burgers

2. Place the ground beef in a bowl and, using your 
hands, work the salt and the pepper into the meat

3. The meat patties shrink as 
they cook, so shape them ½ to 1 inch larger in diameter than the buns

4. Sprinkle 
a little salt over both sides of the patties

5. Grill the patties for 3 minutes or until 
done to your preference

6. Turn the patties, top with the cheese slices and some 
of the caramelized onions, and grill until the cheese has melted and the burgers 
are seared on the second side, about 3 minutes

7. Transfer to a plate and let rest 
for 2 minutes

8. Cut the buns in half and spread the cut sides of both halves with the softened 
butter

9. Place the halves on the grill, buttered-side down, and toast until golden 
brown, about 2 minutes

10. Heat the oil in a deep, heavy pot or an electric deep fryer to 375°F on a deep-
frying thermometer
