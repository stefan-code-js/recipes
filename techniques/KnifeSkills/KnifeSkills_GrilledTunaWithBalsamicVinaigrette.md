## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Per serving: Calories, 450; Protein, 36 g; Fat, 26 g (54% cal.); Cholesterol, 65 mg; Carbohydrates, 14 g; Fiber, 1 g; Sodium, 220 mg.PROCEDURE

2. Make a vinaigrette (see p. 670 for procedure) with the vinegar,

3. Mix in the diced red pepper.

4. Season the tuna steaks with a little salt and brush with a little

5. Grill the steaks until rare or medium rare.

6. For each portion, arrange the onions in the center of the plate.

7. Cut the steak in half to display the interior. arrange the two  halves on top of the onions.

8. Spoon a little vinaigrette around the tuna.
