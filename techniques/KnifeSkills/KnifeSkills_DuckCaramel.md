# Duck Caramel

**Category:** Knife Skills  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

---

## Overview

Duck Caramel 
Wild duck meat and bones are simmered in dashi with dried ceps, 
lemon thyme, juniper wood, and an apple. The broth is strained, 
reduced, and then further reduced with some cep oil to a thick and 
fudgy consistency. 
The reduction is piped decoratively onto a Mexican oregano leaf 
and seasoned with crushed toasted black pepper and salt.
