## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Grilled Preserved Morels 
Maitake mushrooms are dry-aged, coated in koji oil, and cold-smoked

2. The smoked mushrooms are then baked with lemon thyme ant broth 
and Douglas fir ant broth and the resulting maitake broth is reduced 
over a charcoal fire

3. Toasted beechnuts are pounded with kelp salt and dried lacto­
fermented ceps and folded into brown butter emulsion to create 
a paste

4. Morels preserved in koji oil are grilled until crisp, filled with the 
brown butter-beechnut paste, and dusted with mushroom-seaweed 
powder

5. The filled morels are placed in a ramekin, and maitake broth 
and cep oil are poured on top
