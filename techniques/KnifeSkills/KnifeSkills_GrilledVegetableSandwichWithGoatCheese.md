## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Cut the zucchini lengthwise into slices about ¼ in. (6 mm) thick.

2. If the eggplant is large, with a thick skin, peel it. Cut large

3. Core and seed the peppers. Cut into quarters lengthwise.

4. Cut the onion crosswise into slices about 1⁄3 in. (8 mm) thick. hold

5. Brush the vegetables with oil and sprinkle them with salt.

6. Grill the vegetables over medium heat, turning as necessary, until they are tender and lightly grill-marked. Cooking times

7. remove from the grill and brush with a little balsamic vinegar.8 oz 250 g eggplant, trimmed

8. (see step 8) Focaccia 8. Cut the focaccia into 3½ × 5 in. (9 × 13 cm) rectangles. If the focaccia is thick, split the rectangles in half horizontally. If it is thin, use 2 rectangles per portion.

9. arrange the grilled vegetables on half of the rectangles.

10. arrange the tomatoes on top of the vegetables.

11. top with the goat cheese and the rest of the bread rectangles.

12. For service, serve whole or cut in half at an angle.4–6 oz 125–180 g Sun-dried tomatoes, packed
