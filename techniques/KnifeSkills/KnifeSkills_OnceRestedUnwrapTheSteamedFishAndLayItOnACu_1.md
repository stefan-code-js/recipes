# Once rested, unwrap the steamed fish and lay it on a cutting board so that it is lying on its side

**Category:** Knife Skills  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

---

## Overview

Once rested, unwrap the steamed fish and lay it on a cutting board so that it is lying on its side 
with one of the fillets facing you, skin -side up. Use a very sharp knife to slice all the way through 
the side of the fish, being sur e to cut the tail in half. This will yield 2 portions of fish. Lay the fish 
open so that you can see the layers of fish and mousse. Brush each half with fresh barley koji butter and liberally spread the veggie XO sauce across the cut face of each portion of fish.
