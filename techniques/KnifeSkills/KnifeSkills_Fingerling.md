## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. The yield of whey will be approximately half 
the amount of yoghurt

2. Heat the whey slightly and add the butter 
in pieces

3. Smoked fat 
Boil the fat in unsalted water in a large pan for 2 hours~ Cool, keeping 
it under pressure between trays

4. Freeze the fat, then slice it finely on 
a meat slicer

5. Cut out into 13-cm discs

6. Potato puree 
Boil the potatoes until tender

7. Strain off the liquid and leave the 
potatoes in the uncovered pan for a few seconds to release some 
steam

8. Heat the milk, cream and butter in a small pan

9. Pass the 
potatoes through a drum sieve (strainer) into a large bowl and 
combine with the milk mixture
