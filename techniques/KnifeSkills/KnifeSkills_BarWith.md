JUNI -SHAUN HERGATT 
For this dish, which he served at his New York restaurant Juni, chef 
Shaun Hergatt first layered squab and foie gras with kuro powder and star anise, 
then pressed and sliced it to create a marbling effect. "The idea is a classic con­
nection of flavors that's also a spin on a little chocolate bar," says Hergatt; indeed, 
his inspiration was the Rocky Road bars he ate as a child. At Juni, which closed in
