# Training Drill: Searing, Maillard, and Crust Management

## Goal

Learn color development without burning.

## Practice

1. Cut 10 identical beef cubes (4×4×4 cm).

2. Sear each at 190 °C, 200 °C, 210 °C, 220 °C, 230 °C.

3. Measure color, crust thickness, and fond quality.

4. Repeat with chicken skin, duck skin, and fish skin.

5. Record ideal sear temp per protein on your line chart.

## Advanced

- Repeat the test with different fats (clarified butter, grapeseed oil, beef tallow, duck fat) to compare smoke points and taste.
