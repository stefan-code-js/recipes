## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Carrot Terrine 
Carrots are slowly cooked in rhubarb root oil with roasted kelp and 
dried carrot flowers until caramelized and tender

2. The cooked carrots 
are bundled and wrapped tightly to form a roulade, then sliced 
crosswise to create a mosaic-like puck

3. The slice of carrot terrine is 
wrapped with a long piece of kelp that's been braised in aronia juice, 
dried mushrooms, and other aromatics

4. Black currant wood fudge and grilled lemon thyme paste are 
piped into a serving bowl and the wrapped carrot terrine is placed on 
top

5. The carrots are dressed with rose oil, Danish curry powder, and 
salt to finish
