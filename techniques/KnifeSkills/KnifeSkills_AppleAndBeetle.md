## Overview

Apple and Beetle 
A beetle is created by shaping black fruit leather made from dark 
berries and berry juice, black garlic, sugar, and charcoal for color. The 
beetle body is a tiny fruit-leather envelope filled with a semi-dried 
mulberry, black currant bud, coriander seed, and angelica seed. The 
beetle's other parts are made from fruit leather shaped using a template, 
with oxalis leaves layered under the wings. The parts are affixed to the 
body with red currant and rose hip paste.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Apple and Beetle 
A beetle is created by shaping black fruit leather made from dark 
berries and berry juice, black garlic, sugar, and charcoal for color

2. The 
beetle body is a tiny fruit-leather envelope filled with a semi-dried 
mulberry, black currant bud, coriander seed, and angelica seed

3. The 
beetle's other parts are made from fruit leather shaped using a template, 
with oxalis leaves layered under the wings

4. The parts are affixed to the 
body with red currant and rose hip paste

5. Balls of fresh apple are macerated in apple ceviche liquid

6. The top of 
an apple, complete with stem and leaves, is sliced off and reserved

7. The 
apple is hollowed out to form a vessel and half the macerated apple balls 
are placed inside

8. The apple balls are dotted with red currant and rose 
hip paste, then garnished with lemon thyme leaves and sprinkled with 
green coriander seeds, green angelica seeds, and black currant shoots

9. The remaining apple balls are placed on top and dressed with a sauce 
made from the apple macerating liquid blended with rose oil

10. More 
coriander seeds, angelica seeds, and black currant shoots are arranged 
on top and seasoned with elderflower oil
