## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Pheasant Salad 
Lightly cooked egg yolks are cured in beef garum, oyster garum, and 
lacto-fermented cep water, then whisked until smooth with sunflower 
seed and beef garum oil and yeast broth reduction; chicken wing garum 
toasted sunflower seeds are chopped and folded into the paste

2. A ring of the cured egg yolk paste is piped into a serving bowl

3. More garum toasted sunflower seeds are arranged in concentric circles 
over the paste

4. Marigold petals are arranged around the outside of the 
"flower," and some rose petals and salted fresh hazelnuts in hazelnut oil 
are tucked around the perimeter

5. Mixed greens are dressed in Jun-spiced butter and gently grilled, 
then seasoned with horseradish juice and chicken wing garum

6. Fresh 
greens are dressed with juniper rose vinaigrette

7. The grilled greens and 
fresh greens are arranged around the sunflower and the dish is finished 
with ancho chile oil

8. A jug of warmed pheasant koji yeast sauce is served 
at the table
