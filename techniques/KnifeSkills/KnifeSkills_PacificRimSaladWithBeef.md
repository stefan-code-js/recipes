2. Slice the beef about ¼ in. (6 mm) thick, then cut into thin strips.

3. No more than about 2 hours before serving, marinate the beef in the vinaigrette 30–60 minutes.14 fl oz 400 mL Oriental Vinaigrette (p. 672)

4. add the mixed vegetables to the beef and vinaigrette

5. place a bed of shredded Chinese cabbage on salad plates.

6. portion the salad mixture onto the plates.
