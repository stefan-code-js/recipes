## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Apple and Oyster 
Sloeberries are blended with cold-infused jasmine tea and birch syrup

2. The juice is strained and set with gelatin

3. Peeled Gala apples are cooked in apple cooking tea and then cooked 
again with the melted sloe berry marinade

4. The marinade is strained, 
poured over the cooled apples, and allowed to set once more

5. Two spoonfuls of the gelled sloeberry marinade are placed onto 
a serving plate

6. A raw oyster is halved and placed on the gel, and thin 
slices of the cooked apple, brushed with gooseberry praline, are laid 
on top of the oyster halves and seasoned with roasted kelp salt

7. The dish 
is finished with jasmine and gooseberry broth and droplets of fresh 
koji oil
