# Marinated Mussel

**Category:** Knife Skills  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

---

## Overview

Marinated Mussel 
Lightly cooked blue mussel cheeks are stacked and arranged on one 
half of a mussel shell, then seasoned with hazelnut oil and mussel 
cooking liquor. Thinly sliced salted fresh hazelnuts are shingled on top. 
More mussel cheeks are stacked in the other half of the mussel 
shell, seasoned with Douglas fir oil, and garnished with pepper sol, a 
vinegared pine cone, and a few drops of mussel liquor. The mussels are 
served in a bowl of Irish moss.
