## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. 4 fl oz 125 mL Lemon juice

2. to taste to taste hot red pepper sauce

3. or a ïoli II (p. 675)

4. Slice the body sacs of the squid crosswise into rings. Leave

5. Combine the squid with the lemon juice and marinate, refrigerated, 2–3 hours.

6. Mix the flour and salt.

7. Drain the squid and dry on clean towels. Immediately before cooking, toss the squid with the flour, then shake in a large strainer to remove excess flour.

8. Deep-fry at 350°F (175°C) just until lightly golden. remove from the fryer and drain.

9. heat the tomato sauce and season to taste with the hot pepper sauce.

10. heap the fried squid in the center of the serving plates, accompanied by 2 fl oz (60 mL) of each of the sauces in small cups. If desired, top with a little deep-fried parsley.

11. or dip in batter, using one of the batter recipes for Onion Rings (p. 347); the recipe for Beer Batter is recommended.
