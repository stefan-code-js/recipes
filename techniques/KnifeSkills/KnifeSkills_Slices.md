Separate
the

lobes
of
the
foie
gras
and
remove
any
of
the
main
veins
and
exterior
fat
with
tweezers
and
a
paring
knife.
Slice
each
lobe
into
3/4-inch
slices
and
trim
them,

necessary,
to
yield
even
shapes.
Reserve
the
trim
for
the
Rendered
Foie
Gras
Fat.

the
Rendered
Foie
Gras
Fat,
place
the
foie
gras
trim
in
a
sous
vide
bag
and
vacuum-seal.
Place
the
bag
in
a
water
bath
maintained
at
210°F
by
an
im
