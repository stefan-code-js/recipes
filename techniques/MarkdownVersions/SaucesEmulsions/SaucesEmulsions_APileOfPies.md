## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. A Pile of Pies 
Five different pie shells are made: The cream pie shell is simply heavy 
cream into which a frozen pie shell mold is dipped; the frozen cream 
shell is released and popped back into the blast freezer

2. The berry powder cream pie shell is a cream pie shell that's 
completely coated with berry powder

3. The rhubarb emulsion pie shell is made by infusing sweetened 
clarified rhubarb juice with green coriander, quince tea, dried 
roseroot, and sweet licorice syrup, setting the mixture with gelatin, and 
emulsifying with rose oil

4. The mixture is frozen onto a pie mold

5. The raspberry pie shell is made simply of frozen honey-sweetened 
raspberry puree

6. The rhubarb crisp pie shell is made from clarified rhubarb juice, 
a puree of cooked apple skins and flesh, and sugar

7. The mixture is 
spread thin and dried, then circles are stamped out, warmed, and gently 
shaped around pie molds

8. To assemble the dish, a cold rock is placed in the serving dish and 
topped with a tall stack of the pie shells, each arranged slightly offÂ­
center and shingled over the edge of the one below, with mulberry 
cream and rhubarb gel between them to hold the shells together
