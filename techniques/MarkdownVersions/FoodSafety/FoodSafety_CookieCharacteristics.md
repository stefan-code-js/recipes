COOKIE CHARACTERISTICS 
AND THEIR CAUSES
cookies come in an infinite variety of shapes, sizes, flavors, and textures. characteristics that are 
desirable in some are not desirable in others. For example, we want some cookies to be crisp and 
others to be soft. We want some to hold their shape and others to spread during baking. i n order 
to produce the characteristics we want and to correct faults, it is useful to know what causes these 
characteristics.
