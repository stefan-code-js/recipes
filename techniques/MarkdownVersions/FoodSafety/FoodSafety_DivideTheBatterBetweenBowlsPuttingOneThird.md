## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. 295
Divide the batter between 2 bowls, putting one-third  
in one and two-thirds in the other

2. Color the one-third red  
with the raspberry concentrate, and the two-thirds green  
with the pistachio paste.Whisk the egg yolks and sugar in a bowl until pale and thick,  
and then whisk in the cream

3. Sift in the flour and baking powder, 
add the lime zest, and mix together

4. Finally, incorporate  
the melted butter

5. Fill the paper cone with the softened butter and pipe a line  
of butter down the center of the cake

6. Bake for about 15 minutes 
and then lower the temperature to 300°F  (145°C /Gas mark 2 )
