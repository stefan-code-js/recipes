6. In another saucepan, make a dry caramel with the sugar, then remove from the heat and add the milk to stop the cooking process

7. Return the pan with the caramel milk mixture to the heat

8. Combine the 
glucose powder, sucrose, powdered milk, and stabilizer in a small bowl and add to the mixture when it reaches 95°F  (35°C ), then add the butter

9. When the mixture reaches 104°F  (40°C ), add the egg yolk and cook at 
185°F  (85°C ) for 2 minutes
