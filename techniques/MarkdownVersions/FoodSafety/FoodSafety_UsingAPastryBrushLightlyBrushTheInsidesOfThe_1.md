## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. 305
Using a pastry brush, lightly brush the insides of the molds  
with the wax (or you can do this the next day)

2. Remove from the heat, cover with plastic wrap, and allow to cool 
to below 175°F  (80°C )

3. Remove the vanilla bean

4. Preheat the oven to 425°F  (220°C–230°C /Gas mark 7 )

5. Whisk the chilled batter well to make it easier to pour,  
then pour it into the prepared molds: fill them to within ¼ in

6. Bake for about 45  minutes

7. Unmold onto a rack as soon as they are taken out of the oven.3 •  2 • 
6 • CHEFS’ NOTES
The temperature of the milk is crucial when making canelé batter: it should never exceed 175°F  (80°C )  
when it is added to the other ingredients, so check the temperature with a thermometer.
