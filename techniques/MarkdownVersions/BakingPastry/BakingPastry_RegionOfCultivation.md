4. Flowers  are

5. Pods  are graded 
by width, with

The seeds in good-quality cardamom pods 
should be black and slightly sticky

6. Avoid using seeds that are dry and pale

7. BOTANICAL NAME
Elettaria cardamomum
ALSO KNOWN AS
Small cardamom, green cardamom, true 
cardamom, queen of spices

8. MAJOR FLAVOUR COMPOUND
Cineole

9. PARTS USED 
Whole “pods” (fruits) containing seeds.METHOD OF CULTIVATION 
Almost ripe seed pods are harvested by hand five or six times a year

10. COMMERCIAL PREPARATION 
Seed pods are washed and then dried in the sun or in heated “curing” rooms
