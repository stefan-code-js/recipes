## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Waffle Batter  
Fat spray  
Cloudberry Mousse  
7 to 9 Candied Cloudberries  
Preheat the waffle iron in a combi oven at 250째C (100% fan) set to dry heat

2. Pull the pans out,
and double check the internal temperature of the waffle iron, on both plates, which should besitting at around 220째C

3. Transfer the iron to a BBQ raised slightly above the coals to avoid largeflareups

4. Spray both sides of the waffle iron with fat spray to coat them and pipe approximately150 g of waffle batter into the hot waffle iron

5. Close the iron, and flip it over immediately, setting a timer for 90 seconds

6. During that 90 -second cooking time, the waffle iron should be
flipped 2 or 3 times to ensure even cooking

7. Mix the cloudberry mousse ingredients together,spoon 70 g of the mousse into a ramekin, and top it with the candied cloudberries

8. Remove the
waffle  from the pan once cooked, crispy, and well caramelized

9. Transfer the waffle to a napkin -
lined plate and  serve immediately with the mousse
