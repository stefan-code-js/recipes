## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. 100 RAZOR CLAM, 
BEET, PUFFED 
WHEAT& 
HORSERADISH 
SAUCE 
THE WIZARD'S COOKBOOK RONNY EMBORG 
The complementary relationship between red and green provides a 
striking focal point for this dish in chef Ronny Emborg's The Wizard's Cookbook

2. The 
beet elements create an off-kilter frame for a vibrant green smear of dill oil, while 
a cluster of puffed grains offers benign but pronounced textural contrast

3. To plate 
the dish, Emborg placed razor clam atop bands of beet that he then rolled up like 
cannelloni and garnished with sago grains and dill

4. After arranging puffed wheat 
next to one of the "cannelloni," Emborg placed some dill emulsion on the plate and 
topped it with a disc of frozen beetroot juice

5. Then he added horseradish sauce to 
the plate and split it with dill oil

6. The shapes and their placement evoke a Kandinsky 
canvas-geometrical, abstract, and bold
