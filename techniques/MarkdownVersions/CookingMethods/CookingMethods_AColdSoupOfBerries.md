## Overview

A Cold Soup of Berries 
Cherry tomatoes are peeled, seeded, and hollowed, then filled with 
semi-dried tomatoes. Raspberries are filled with a strawberry paste 
made from fresh strawberries deeply caramelized in koji oil and 
seasoned with roseroot spice blend. Fresh blueberries are filled with 
lemon thyme flowers. 
The tomatoes, raspberries, and blueberries are grouped in a serving 
bowl and berry soup is poured around them. The soup is then seasoned 
with black currant wood and morita chile o

## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. A Cold Soup of Berries 
Cherry tomatoes are peeled, seeded, and hollowed, then filled with 
semi-dried tomatoes

2. Raspberries are filled with a strawberry paste 
made from fresh strawberries deeply caramelized in koji oil and 
seasoned with roseroot spice blend

3. Fresh blueberries are filled with 
lemon thyme flowers

4. The tomatoes, raspberries, and blueberries are grouped in a serving 
bowl and berry soup is poured around them

5. The soup is then seasoned 
with black currant wood and morita chile oils

6. The dish is garnished 
with grilled lemon thyme
