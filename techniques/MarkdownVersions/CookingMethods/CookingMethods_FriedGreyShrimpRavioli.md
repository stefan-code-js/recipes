## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Fried Grey Shrimp Ravioli 
Sea lettuce is laid out, liberally lubricated with parsley-koji oil, and 
marinated under vacuum

2. Freeze-dried gooseberries are steamed with koji oil, maitake garum, 
freeze-dried rhubarb, dashi, lemon verbena, and lemon thyme to confit

3. Lightly cooked egg yolks are cured in beef garum and smoked 
seaweed shoyu, then whisked until smooth to make a paste

4. Fjord shrimp are dredged in a seasoned mix of flours, fried until 
crisp, and seasoned with Jun spice mix

5. A square sea lettuce sheet is spread with the cured egg yolk paste 
and then piped with ancho chile fudge

6. Two fried shrimp and two confit 
gooseberry halves are placed on the fudge and the sea lettuce is folded 
over to make a packet

7. The packet is trimmed and brushed with more 
parsley-koji oil
