2. use only as much water as is necessary.

3. Cook beets whole and unpeeled, with root and an inch of stem attached, to protect color. skins

4. When steaming, use solid pans instead of perforated pans to retain the red juices.

5. Whenever possible, serve the cooking liquid as a sauce with the vegetable.

6. Cook uncovered to allow plant acids to escape.

7. Cook for the shortest possible time. Properly cooked green vegetables are tender-crisp, not mushy.
