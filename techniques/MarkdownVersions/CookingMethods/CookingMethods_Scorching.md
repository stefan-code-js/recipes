Scorching
Scorching occurs when milk that is being heated coagulates on the bottom of the pan due to high 
heat. This deposit is likely to burn if cooking continues.
To avoid scorching, heat milk in a double boiler, steamer, or steam-jacketed kettle rather than 
over direct heat.
