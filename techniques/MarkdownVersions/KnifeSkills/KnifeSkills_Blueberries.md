## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Add the puree, the fresh berries and the spruce shoots, blend and 
strain

2. Heat a very small amount of the berry mixture

3. Bloom the 
gelatine, then dissolve into the warm mixture and add to the rest

4. Place in Paco containers in the refrigerator to set

5. Spruce ice cream 
Whisk together the sugar, trimoline and egg yolks

6. Heat the cream, 
milk and milk powder in a pan to 80°C (178°F) and pour it over the 
egg mixture

7. Pour back into the pans and cook, stirring, to make a 
custard

8. Bloom the gelatine, add and cool

9. When the mixture is 
cold, blend the spruce shoots into it and strain
