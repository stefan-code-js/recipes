## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. BearBaba 
Lightly cooked egg yolks are cured in beef garum, then whisked until 
smooth and seasoned with whisky vinegar, Danish whisky, smoked 
butter, more beef garum, and salt to make a sauce

2. Roasted bear meat is simmered in dashi with dried ceps, lemon 
thyme, juniper wood, and apples

3. The broth is strained, reduced, and 
then further reduced with cep oil to a thick and fudgy caramel

4. A bear consomme is made from chicken stock and dashi clarified 
with brined bear meat, egg whites, and celery, and finished with rice 
fava shoyu and chicken wing garum

5. The consomme is reduced by half

6. Chickens and ducks are simmered to make a stock, which is strained 
and left to settle

7. The fat is skimmed off the surface of the stock and 
reserved

8. The stock is reduced to a light glaze

9. The glaze is then mixed 
with some of the reserved fat, brought to a boil, and left to cool until a 
skin forms on top

10. The skin is delicately transferred from the saucepan 
to parchment and the parchment is placed in a skillet, where the skin is 
cooked until it begins to crisp but is still malleable
