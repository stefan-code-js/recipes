## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. 583
When the ganache has cooled to 86°F (30°C), stir in the butter

2. Pour onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper  
and allow to set for 1 hour.Put the chopped chocolate in a bowl and strain the hot cream 
over it, stirring gently until you have a smooth ganache

3. As soon as you have dipped them, roll the truffles in the cocoa 
powder with the aid of a dipping fork, until they are coated

4. Allow to set for 1 hour and then carefully shake the truffles  
in a sifter to remove excess cocoa

5. 3 •  2 • 
6 • CHEFS’ NOTES
• To coat the truffles in two layers of chocolate, dip them in the tempered chocolate and allow to set

6. Continue as shown in steps 5–6 , dipping the truffles a second time and rolling them in cocoa powder.
