## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Beet Sashimi 
Golden beets are roasted, inf used with rose oil, and then sliced into 
thin fans

2. Freeze-dried black currants, dried ceps, sol, and Madagascar 
pepper are dehydrated and then ground into a fine powder, which is 
blended with black currant wood oil, marigold oil, and kanzuri oil to 
make a praline

3. Three rings of thinly sliced fresh plum are arranged in a serving 
bowl

4. The centers of the rings are filled with berry praline and the edges 
of the rings are dotted with vegetable fudge, pollen paste, and ant paste

5. Semi-dried cloudberries and semi-dried lacto-fermented mirabelle 
plums are tucked alongside

6. Three beet fans are placed on top of the plum clusters, beet sauce is 
spooned around, and the dish is finished with sprays of whisky vinegar 
and small drops of rose oil and morita chile oil
