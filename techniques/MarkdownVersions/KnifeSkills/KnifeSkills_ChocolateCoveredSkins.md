## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. drop the temperature of the mixture to 28°C

2. From ther e, place the bowl over a double boiler 
and bring the temperature back up to 30°C

3. Once up to temperature, hold it at 30°C

4. CHOCOLATE -COVERED SKINS  
1 fried Cod Skin  
Tempered Chocolate  
Fennel seeds, toasted  
Arctic thyme  
Raw licorice powder  
Flake sea salt  
Remove the fried skins from the dehydrator and allow them to cool down to room temperature

5. Using tweezers, submerge the skins into the tempered chocolate in a single dipping and then 
pull the skin out allowing it drip off completely

6. Transfer it to a tray  lined with parchment paper

7. As the chocolate is setting, sprinkle it with some of the toasted fennel seeds, arctic thyme, a tiny
bit of the licorice powder, and salt

8. Reserve in an airtight container in the fridge for service

9. TO SERVE  
1 Chocolate -Cover ed Skin  
1 dagger  
When called out, remove the chocolate -covered skin from the fridge, plate it, and serve it with a
dagger

10. One skin will be cut in half by the guest to yield 2 portions
