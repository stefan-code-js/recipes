## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Apple and Trout Roe 
Lacto-fermented plums are glazed with a reduction of mushroom-kelp 
broth and beef garum, dried until chewy, and cut into tiny dice

2. Maitake 
mushrooms are cold-smoked and lightly roasted in brown butter until 
crispy, then dehydrated and finely chopped into a crumble

3. The lacto-fermented plums and cold-smoked maitakes are 
combined with cooked grains, pickled chanterelles, and a stew base 
made by emulsifying butter into muddled green coriander seed, roasted 
kelp salt, celery and fennel reductions, and lacto plum and tomato 
reduction

4. A tiny dice of fresh apple is folded into this ragout

5. A lightly steamed whole apple is hollowed out and filled with the 
ragout

6. The layer of ragout is topped with trout roe that has been lightly 
cured in kelp and trout bushi dashi

7. The top of the apple is repositioned, 
and the apple is lightly steamed
