## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Mince the tuna with a knife. the most effective way to do

2. Mixed the chopped tuna with the remaining ingredients shortly before serving, seasoning to taste with salt and pepper. Do not mix too long in advance, as the acidity of the lime juice coagulates the protein as for seviche (p. 661).

3. tuna tartare may be served very simply mounded on a small plate or in more elaborate ways (see variations).
