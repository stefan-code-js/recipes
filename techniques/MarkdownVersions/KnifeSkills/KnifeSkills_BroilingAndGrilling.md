## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. as needed as needed Lemon wedges

2. Season the steaks with salt and pepper.

3. place the oil or melted butter in a small pan. Dip both sides

4. place on the rack of a preheated broiler. Broil under medium

5. Complete the cooking on the second side.

6. plate the fish. place a slice of seasoned butter on top of  each steak. Garnish the plate with a lemon wedge. Serve

7. Per serving: Calories, 210; Protein, 32 g; Fat, 6 g (27% cal.); Cholesterol, 125 mg; Carbohydrates, 5 g; Fiber, 1 g; Sodium, 170 mg.PROCEDURE

8. In a nonreactive container, mix together the lemon juice,  salt, white pepper, and cayenne.

9. place the fish fillets in the mixture, turning them to coat all sides. Let stand 15 minutes.

10. remove the fish from the marinade. Dip the fillets in the oil and remove, letting the excess oil drip off.

11. Grill the fillets on a preheated, seasoned grill or broiler. Grill until just done. Do not overcook, or the fish will be dry.

12. plate the fillets. Next to each fillet, place 1½ oz (50 g) fruit  salsa.
