## Overview

137
COCKSCOMB
1/2
pound
cockscombs
1
clove
garlic,
crushed
but
kept
whole
5
sprigs
thyme
4
cups
duck
fat
Preheat
the
oven
to
300°F.
Rinse
the
cockscombs
to
remove
any
blood.
Pat
dry
and
place
in
a
medium
pan
with
the
garlic
and
thyme.
Melt
the
duck
fat
in
a
large
saucepan
over
low
heat.
Pour
over
the
cockscombs
and
cover
the
pan.
Place
in
the
oven
for
3
hours,
until
tender.
Cool
to
room
temperature
in
the
fat.
Once
cool,
strain,
discarding
the
garlic,
thyme,
and
duck
fat.
Dice
(1/8
inch)
the
coc
