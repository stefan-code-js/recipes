## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. 77
Flatten the centers of the disks (not the edges) with the rolling 
pin to make them oval

2. Brush the pastry edges with egg wash  
so they can be sealed later in step 4

3. Turn the parcels over, place on a baking sheet and brush  
with egg wash

4. Chill for 30  minutes and then brush again

5. Preheat the oven to 400° F (200°C /Gas mark  6)

6. With a small knife, mark  lines in a feather pattern on top of each 
turnover and pierce 5–6  small holes so steam can escape.Put the turnovers in the oven and reduce the temperature  
to 375° F (190°C /Gas mark  5)

7. Bake for about 30  minutes  
until golden brown

8. As soon as they come out of the oven,  
brush with the syrup
