## Overview

HAND-CUT TAJARIN 
Tajarin, pronounced " tie-yar-EEN," is Piemontese dialect for “tagliarini.” It's a whisper- 
thin, hand-cut noodle that’s deceptively difficult because the goal is to make them as 
thin and uniform as possible. Since they're so diminutive and lithe, they're best with the 
lightest kiss of sauce—think sage browned butter.
