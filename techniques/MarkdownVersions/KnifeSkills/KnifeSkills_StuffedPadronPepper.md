# Stuffed Padron Pepper

**Category:** Knife Skills  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

---

## Overview

Stuffed Padron Pepper 
Padron peppers are charred over hot charcoal and peeled. Some are 
seeded and reserved for a filling, and the rest are kept intact (including 
their stems) and seeded through an incision on one side. 
Red bell peppers are also charred and peeled and then cut into 
quarters and dried. The semi-dried bell peppers and the seeded 
charred padr6n peppers are chopped to make a filling. 
Fava beans are peeled and blanched and then seasoned with a 
parsley-spruce wood oil marinade

## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Stuffed Padron Pepper 
Padron peppers are charred over hot charcoal and peeled

2. Some are 
seeded and reserved for a filling, and the rest are kept intact (including 
their stems) and seeded through an incision on one side

3. Red bell peppers are also charred and peeled and then cut into 
quarters and dried

4. The semi-dried bell peppers and the seeded 
charred padr6n peppers are chopped to make a filling

5. Fava beans are peeled and blanched and then seasoned with a 
parsley-spruce wood oil marinade

6. The favas are mixed with the 
chopped pepper filling, bell pepper marinade, and parsley, and stuffed 
into the whole peeled padr6n peppers, along with semi-dried cherry 
tomato halves, confit gooseber ry halves, semi-dried lacto-fermented 
plum pieces, fresh coriander seeds, and lemon thyme

7. The incision in 
the stuffed padr6n pepper is sealed with a lemon verbena leaf and the 
front side of the pepper is brushed with smoked parsley puree
