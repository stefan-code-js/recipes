## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Black Currant Wood  Oil* 
In service, open the queen clam, clean it lightly, and fillet it in half

2. Clean and harvest the roe 
from the shell and cut it into small slices

3. Place a smear of vegetable fudge over each half, and a 
slice of the roe over each half, and season with salt

4. Place the 2 pieces back into the cleaned 
shell, side by side, and replace the top of the shell

5. Put it on the first ice plate  
Carefully open the mahogany clam shell with a turning knife and remove the clam

6. Clean the clam by removing the skirts and guts and slice the muscle thin

7. Reserve one of the larger adductor muscles

8. Clean the adductor muscle and slice it about 2 mm thick

9. Place the sliced clam back into its cleaned shell, and top it with the slice of adductor muscle, offset to the right.Top t he clam with 4 triangular slices of salted green gooseberry

10. Top each slice of the salted 
green gooseberries with a picked small leaf of pickled black currant shoots
