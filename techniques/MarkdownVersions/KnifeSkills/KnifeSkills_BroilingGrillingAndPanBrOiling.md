## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Review this material carefully before beginning this

2. Preheat an iron skillet over a high flame until it is very

3. If desired, brush the meat lightly with oil. Place the meat in the pan, presentation side down. Adjust the heat as necessary for proper cooking. Thin cuts should be cooked over very high heat in order to create enough browning in the short time they will cook. Thicker items should be cooked over slightly lower heat so that they will not brown too much during the longer cooking time.3. During cooking, pour off fat that accumulates in the pan, if necessary.

4. When the bottom of the meat is brown and the meat is cooked halfway, turn it over.

5. Cook the second side until the meat is cooked to the desired doneness.

6. Remove from the pan and serve immediately.
