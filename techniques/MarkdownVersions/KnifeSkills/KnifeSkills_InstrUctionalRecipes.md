## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Collect and measure all ingredients. Refrigerate eggs, cheese, milk, and chicken at 40°F or lower until needed.

2. Collect all equipment.

3. Place the flour in the hotel pan. Season with the salt and white pepper.

4. Break the eggs into the stainless-steel bowl and discard the shells. Beat with the wire whip until foamy. Add the grated cheese and milk.

5. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate at below 40°F until needed.

6. Flatten the chicken breasts lightly with the meat mallet until ½ in. thick. Place the breasts in a hotel pan.

7. Clean and sanitize the mallet and the work surface. Wash hands thoroughly.

8. Place one of the sauté pans over moderate heat. Allow to heat 2 minutes.

9. Measure 1 oz clarified butter into the pan.

10. One at a time, dip 3 chicken breasts in the seasoned flour until completely coated on both sides. Shake off excess. Dip in the egg mixture. Coat both sides completely. Return remaining chicken and egg mixture to refrigerator.
