## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Fava Beans and Berries 
Lacto-fermented koji water, smoked tomato water, butter, and rose oil 
are blended to make rose sauce

2. Fava beans are grilled in their pods, then the inner beans are peeled, 
split into tender green discs, and marinated in parsley oil

3. Cherry tomatoes, brushed with koji oil and moistened with honey, 
are dried until dense but tender and chewy

4. The favas and slices of cherry, gooseberry, strawberry, and dried 
tomato are arranged on a serving plate in overlapping concentric rings

5. Golden raspberry halves, wild cherries, and wild strawberries are 
scattered on top and parsley puree is dotted around

6. Soft herbs and 
flowers are strewn over the favas and fruits, along with coriander seeds, 
black currant shoots, and a few tiny mounds of pollen and morita oil

7. The dish is sprinkled with ants and finished with rose sauce and 
drops of parsley oil
