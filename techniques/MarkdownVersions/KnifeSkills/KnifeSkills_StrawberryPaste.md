## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Stuff the 
hollowed cherry tomatoes with 4 pieces of semi- dried cherry tomatoes, and likewise, fill the 
raspberries cavities with the strawberry paste until completely full

2. Slice the top off the blueberries and hollow them out with a 10 mm parisienne scoop, being sure to leave a small amount of flesh in there

3. Fill the resulting holes with the lemon thyme flowers

4. In a bowl, arrange all the tomatoes, raspberries,  and 3 blueberries tightly together against each other at 
the bottom of the bowl

5. Place 2 blueberries over the other fruits to build some height

6. Then, around the fruit, add approximately 25 g of the berry soup from a squeeze bottle

7. Garnish the soup with a good amount of the grilled lemon thyme

8. Season the soup with a bit of salt, and drizzle in 1 pipette of black currant wood oil and 8 drops of morita chile oil
