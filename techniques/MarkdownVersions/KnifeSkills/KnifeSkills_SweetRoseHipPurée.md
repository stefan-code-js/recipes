## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. 3 Brined Japanese Quince* quarters (made using an 8% salt brine)  
Sweet Rose Hip Purée  
Pollen Mix  
Take the pe eled, seeded rose hip out of the fridge, and pipe the Japanese quince paste into the 
hole, filling it halfway, then add a sprinkling of salt

2. Continue to fill the hole with the sweet rose
hip purée

3. Brush the rose hip with rose oil and top it with 3 quarte rs of the brined Japanese 
quince

4. In between the brined Japanese quince slices, add 3 small piles of the pollen mix

5. Brushthe bottom of the resin bowl with a bit of rose oil to prevent the rose hip from sticking

6. Place the rose hip in the center of the resin bowl and serve with the “ Duck Caramel ” and the “Potato
and Pumpkin  Tart.”
