5. EMULSION
A smooth, uniform blend of two liquids that do not naturally mix, such as oil and water

6. Making an emulsion requires vigorous hand whisking or blending in a food processor or blender

7. This breaks one of the liquids down into tiny droplets and disperses it throughout the other liquid, forming a temporary suspension

8. Adding an emulsifier like egg yolk stabilizes the emulsion

9. ÉTUVE (DRYING OR STEAMER OVEN)
Mainly found in professional kitchens, this oven can be set  to a wide range of very precise temperatures, rising to a maximum of 572°F (300°C )

10. It is used for drying or steaming delicate
