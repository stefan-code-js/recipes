8

SCALLOPS

scallops
1
teaspoon
salt
Season
the

with

salt.
Steam
the
scallops

combination
steam

185°F.

to

Trim

the

lops

thinner
pieces.
With

1
1/4-inch
ring
cutter,

out
circles
from
the
scallops.
Reserve
all
trim
for
the
Scallop
Salad
and
keep

scallops
on
ice

ready
