## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. heat the oil in a soup pot over moderate heat.

2. add onion, garlic, bell pepper, and green chile. Sweat

3. add the stock. Bring to a boil and skim carefully.

4. add the tomatoes, chickpeas, and corn. Simmer

5. Shortly before serving, add the cooked green beans.  (Beans should be cooked separately and added at the  end so their color won’t be destroyed by the acidity of  the tomatoes.)

6. add salt and white pepper to taste. a dd hot pepper

7. to serve, ladle a portion into soup plates and sprinkle

8. Separate and peel the cloves.

9. piquant Vegetable soup
