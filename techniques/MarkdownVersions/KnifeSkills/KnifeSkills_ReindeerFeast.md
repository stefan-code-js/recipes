## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Reindeer Feast 
Sweetbreads are cooked with smoked koji oil, toasted hay, Douglas fir, 
and lemon thyme, then cold-smoked over Douglas fir

2. Reindeer marrow 
bones are marinated

3. A cluster of white currants is glazed with honey 
and seasoned with chicken wing garum, spruce wood oil, and a salted 
green gooseberry seed

4. A pickled pine cone is brushed with ant paste 
and skewered with a pine shoot

5. Reindeer tongue is sauteed in smoked butter and lemon thyme, and 
seasoned with coriander and gooseberry paste and black pepper

6. The 
marinated bone marrow is seared in butter and skewered with a pine 
shoot

7. When it's time to serve the dish, the sweetbreads are dredged in 
tempura batter and fried, brushed with sweetbread glaze, seasoned 
with sumac spice mix, and speared onto a pine shoot

8. The reindeer tongue is drizzled with reindeer tongue spiced butter 
and speared with a pine shoot

9. A fresh cep is seared and glazed with mushroom glaze, dressed with 
Douglas fir oil, and skewered on a pine shoot

10. A serving bowl is lined with reindeer moss
