## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Sea Snail Salad 
Dried shrimp are cooked in butter with spices, then ground in a mortar 
and pestle vvith garlic, dried tomato and lemon thyme powders, kelp 
salt, and fennel reduction to make a paste

2. The shrimp paste is blended 
with freeze-dried rhubarb, cucumber-kelp broth, fried herbs, chile no 
chile powder, and koji oil to create a rhubarb paste

3. se·a snails are cooked in koji oil with arctic thyme, then sliced very 
thin

4. The snail slices are spread with the rhubarb paste and seasoned 
with chicken wing garum, horseradish juice, and hot chile no chile 
butter

5. The slices are then tossed with a selection of herbs spritzed with 
pine vinegar

6. The salad is arranged in a beeswax bowl and topped with 
more herbs and pickled rose petals that have been brushed with a dashi 
reduction
