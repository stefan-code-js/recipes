## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Caul if lower Waffle 
A buckwheat batter is piped onto a waffle iron to create nicely browned 
waffles

2. Thin slices of cauliflower florets are arranged on parchment in 
concentric rings to create a circle

3. The cauliflower is then seasoned with 
salted green gooseberry brine and Douglas fir oil

4. The waffle is drizzled with Douglas fir oil and then spread with a 
thin layer of vegetable fudge and dotted with parsley and lovage paste 
and ancho chile paste

5. Two ants are placed on each dot of herb paste 
and a half sea buckthorn berry on each chile paste dot

6. The waffle is flipped onto the cauliflower florets and gently pressed 
to join the two, then all is flipped again so the cauliflower is visible 
on top

7. Fennel and coriander blossoms are arranged on top of the 
cauliflower
