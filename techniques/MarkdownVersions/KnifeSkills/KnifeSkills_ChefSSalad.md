## Step-by-Step Instructions

2. arrange the turkey, ham, and cheese strips neatly on top

3. arrange the remaining items attractively on the salad.

4. hold for service. If salads must be held for over 1 hour, they should be covered so the meats and cheese don’t dry out.

5. Serve with any appropriate salad dressing on the side in a separate container.12 oz 350 g turkey breast, cut into thin strips

6. Roasted Beet Salad with Gorgonzola

7. Wrap the red beets in foil. Wrap the yellow beets in a separate

8. Bake at 400°F (200 °C) until the beets are tender, about 1 hour.

9. Cool the beets slightly. trim the root and stem ends, and pull off

10. Cut the red beets crosswise into thin slices.

11. Cut the yellow beets vertically into quarters.1 lb 8 oz 750 g yellow beets, small

12. arrange the sliced red beets in circles on cold salad plates.

13. toss the mesclun with half the vinaigrette.

14. Drizzle the remaining vinaigrette over the sliced beets.

15. place a small mound of greens in the center of each plate.

16. arrange the quartered yellow beets around the greens.

17. Sprinkle the tops of the salads with the crumbled gorgonzola.12 fl oz 375 mL Mustard Vinaigrette (p. 670)
