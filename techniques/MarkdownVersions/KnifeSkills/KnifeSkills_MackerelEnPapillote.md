## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Cut out a piece of parchment in a heart shape, as shown in

2. (Foil may be used instead of parchment.) the piece must be big enough to hold the fish and still have room for crimping the

3. place the fillet on one side of the heart. Brush with melted butter and

4. Fold and crimp the parchment, as shown in the illustration, to enclose the fish tightly.

5. place the folded package in a sauté pan or, if several orders are being done at once, on a sheet pan. Set on the range to start the cooking.

6. as soon as the paper begins to puff, place the pan in a hot oven (450°F/230°C). Bake until the parchment is puffed and browned, 5–8 minutes. (If the paper doesn’t brown, you may run it under the broiler for a second.)

7. Serve immediately. the parchment should be cut open in front of the customer.
