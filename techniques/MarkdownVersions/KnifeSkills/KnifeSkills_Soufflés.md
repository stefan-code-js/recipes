## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. base—usually a heavy béchamel sauce.

2. flavor ingredient—cheese, vegetables, seafood, etc.

3. egg whites, beaten.

4. Well risen above the rim of the baking dish.

5. the top should be level, not tilted; that is, the soufflé should be evenly risen on all sides.

6. Moist interior.

7. Light, puffy texture.

8. properly seasoned.À LA CARTE  SERVICE

9. Prepare a heavy béchamel sauce.

10. Combine the sauce with egg yolks.

11. Prepare the flavor ingredients—grate cheese, cook and chop vegetables, and so on.

12. Combine the base and the flavor ingredients.

13. Beat egg whites and fold in.

14. Bake in a soufflé dish that has been buttered and dusted with parmesan cheese.

15. Serve immediately.
