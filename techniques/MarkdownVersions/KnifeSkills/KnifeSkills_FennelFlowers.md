## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Fennel flowers  
Pick the flowers from our garden fresh daily

2. Be sure to pick the flowers while the sun is still up; 
otherwise, some will be closed

3. Be gentle and reserve carefully in containers lined with C -fold 
towel s

4. TO BUILD THE CHOCOLATE SHARD  
Caramelized Chocolate, tempered  
Crispy Bee Larvae  Flowers  
Spread the tempered chocolate onto a Silpat lined with two thin strips of Silpats lining the edgesof both sides, 15 cm apart, to act as risers

5. Use a large offs et pallet knife to spread the chocolate
flat

6. Work with half a Silpat at a time as this process is time sensitive

7. Generously cover the faceof the chocolate with the crispy bee larvae, then the fennel flowers, then the oxalis flowers, andfinally the thym e flowers, working quickly in a team if necessary to stick everything into the 
chocolate before it sets

8. Once garnished and set, crack the sheet of chocolate into a roughly 15
 x
8
 cm shard to share between 2 guests

9. TO SERVE  
1 Chocolate Shard, dressed  
Sweet licorice syrup  
Beeswax Syrup  
Rose Oil*  
Season the chocolate shard with 20 dots of sweet licorice syrup, drizzle one whole pipette of thebeeswax syrup, and 1 whole pipette of rose oil

10. Serve in a wicker basket with a dagger
