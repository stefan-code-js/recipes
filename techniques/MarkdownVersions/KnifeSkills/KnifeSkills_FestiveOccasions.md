## Overview

FESTIVE OCCASIONS
Birthdays can be celebrated with all types of desserts, but certain occasions in France traditionally call 
for a specific cake. For example, for many French people it would not be Epiphany without a galette des 
rois (kings’ cake) or Christmas without a bûche de Noël  (Yule log). To anyone who has grown up eating 
such cakes, the flavors are deeply familiar and steeped in memories of special times spent with family and friends. Tradition can be delicious!
