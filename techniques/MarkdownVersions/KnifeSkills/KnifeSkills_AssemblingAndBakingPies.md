## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Baked pies.

2. Unbaked pies. Baked pie shells are filled with a prepared filling, chilled, and served when the filling is firm

3. Use mealy dough for bottom crusts. Mealy dough absorbs less liquid than flaky dough.

4. Use high bottom heat, at least at the beginning of baking, to set the crust quickly. Bake the pies at the bottom of the oven.

5. do not add hot fillings to unbaked crusts.

6. Use dark metal pie tins, which absorb heat. (if you use disposable aluminum pans, choose pans with the bottoms colored black.)PROCEDURE for Rolling Pie Dough and Lining Pans
