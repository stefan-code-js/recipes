# Sunflower Seed Risotto, Preserved Lemon

## Components

- Soaked sunflower kernels

- Onion base

- Vegetable jus

- Preserved lemon brunoise

- Dill oil

## Method

- Simmer kernels to al dente.

- Finish with vegetable jus.

- Fold in preserved lemon and dill oil.

## Parameters

- **Kernel Finish**: Al dente

## Creative Pairing

A creamy, grain-free risotto using sunflower seeds for texture, brightened by the intense salt-acid of preserved lemon and the fresh perfume of dill oil.
