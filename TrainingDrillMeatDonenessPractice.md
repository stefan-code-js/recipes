# Training Drill: Meat Doneness Practice

## Goal

Train senses to recognize exact internal temperatures by touch, color, and juice.

## Practice

1. Cook six small beef medallions side-by-side to specific core temperatures:

- 48 °C (rare)

- 52 °C (med-rare)

- 55 °C (medium)

- 60 °C (med-well)

- 65 °C (well)

- 70 °C (dry)

1. Slice each. Study texture, juice color, and resistance to the tongs.

2. **Benchmark**: After 20 runs, cooks should guess within ±2 °C by touch.
