# Mackerel, Light Cure, Torch, Mustard-Dill

## Prep & Cook

- **Prep**: Cure **2% salt + 1% sugar 15 min**; wipe (Quick Cure).

- **Cook**: Torch skin to blister.

## Finish

- Mustard-dill oil.

- Pickled cucumber.

## Parameters

- **Cure Salt/Sugar**: 2% / 1%

- **Cure Time**: 15 min

## Plating Cues

- **Texture**: Blistered skin.
