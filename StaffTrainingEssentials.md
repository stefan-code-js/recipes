# Staff Training Essentials

## HACCP and Safety

- Teach cooks that “HACCP” isn’t paperwork — it’s don’t kill guests.

- Everyone knows core temps by heart.

## Precision and Consistency

- Portion every plate; use correct ladle or scoop size.

- Taste everything.

- Write times and temps; no guessing.

## Cleaning Schedule

- **Daily**: Boards, slicers, handles, gaskets, drains, fryer filters.

- **Weekly Deep Clean**: Hoods, oven fans, condenser coils.
