# Training Drill: Bread, Starch & Pastry Texture

## Goal

Perfect internal moisture and crisp ratios.

## Practice: Bread

1. Bake identical loaves at 170, 180, 190, 200 °C.

2. Slice cross-sections; note crumb color and crust thickness.

## Practice: Pasta

1. Cook pasta to 60%, 80%, 100% time.

2. Weigh before/after to see water absorption %.

## Practice: Pastry Control (Sablé)

1. Make sablé with 30%, 40%, 50% butter.

2. Bake all; note spread, crunch, melt.

3. **Action**: Decide your base ratio for consistent tart shells.
