# Sumac Kombucha

## Ingredients

- 2.5 L water

- 250 g wild sumac

- Sugar (for sweetening a portion)

- Starter kombucha (backslop)

- SCOBY

## Process

1. Cold-blend water and sumac; fridge-infuse 4 h.

2. Fine-strain, then strain through a moistened towel (for filtration finesse).

3. Sweeten a portion, recombine, backslop, and add SCOBY.

4. Ferment 7–10 d to target pH.

5. Freeze to hold.

## Parameters

- **Temperature**: 30 °C (ferment)

- **Time**: 4 h (infuse), 7–10 days (ferment)

- **pH**: 3.5 (target)

## Sensory Cues

- **Stop Cue**: pH 3.5.
