# Turbot on the Bone, Beurre Blanc, Dill Oil

## Prep & Cook

- **Prep**: Dry-age 1–2 d on rack.

- **Cook**: Roast on bone 180 °C to **48–50 °C core**.

## Sauce & Finish

- **Sauce**: Classic beurre blanc (Beurre Blanc / Monté technique).

- **Finish**: Dill oil drizzle.

## Parameters

- **Dry Age**: 1–2 d

- **Roast Temp**: 180 °C

- **Core Temp**: 48–50 °C

## Plating Cues

- **Texture**: Gelatin release, nacreous flakes.
