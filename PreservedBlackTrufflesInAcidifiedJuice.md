# Preserved Black Truffles in Acidified Juice

## Ingredients

- Black truffles

- Truffle juice

- Apple balsamic (for acidification)

- 60% ethanol (for sanitizing)

## Process

1. Sanitize jars with 60% ethanol.

2. Adjust truffle juice to pH 4.3 with apple balsamic.

3. Cover truffles in jars, leaving 1.5 cm headspace.

4. Steam 100 °C for 30 min.

5. Hold 30 min more in oven off.

6. Cool and refrigerate; always use sterile tools.

## Parameters

- **Temperature**: 100 °C (steam)

- **Time**: 30 min (steam), 30 min (hold)

- **pH**: 4.3 (target for juice)

- **Headspace**: 1.5 cm

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: Shelf-stable preserved truffles.
