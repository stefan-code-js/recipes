# Menu Engineering Simulation and Optimization

## Ranking Criteria

1. **Popularity**: Sales volume.

2. **Profitability**: Gross profit per item.

## Menu Matrix

- **Star**: High profit + high sales → **Promote**.

- **Plowhorse**: Low profit + high sales → **Raise price slightly or portion smaller**.

- **Puzzle**: High profit + low sales → **Rename or re-plate**.

- **Dog**: Low profit + low sales → **Remove**.

## Review

- Re-evaluate monthly to keep the menu profitable.

- Track changes: Change one recipe (portion, garnish, or price), then track the next two weeks.
