# Yellow Peaso (Pea Miso)

## Ingredients

- 1 kg cooked yellow split peas

- 400 g barley koji

- 40 g sea salt

## Process

1. Mash peas; mix with koji and salt.

2. Pack tightly into jar; cover surface with salt layer.

3. Weight and seal.

4. Ferment 3–6 months.

## Parameters

- **Salt %**: 4%

- **Temperature**: Room temperature (fermentation)

- **Time**: 3–6 months

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: nutty, rich paste—excellent in emulsions or soups.

## Creative Application

Whisk a small amount of the peaso into a brown butter emulsion to finish a dish of roasted root vegetables.
