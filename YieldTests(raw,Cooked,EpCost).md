# Yield Tests (Raw, Cooked, EP Cost)

## Raw Yield Test

- **Purpose**: Find usable meat after trimming.

- **Calculation**: 1 kg whole beef tenderloin @ €25/kg → 0.8 kg usable → yield = 80 %.

- **EP Cost (Edible Portion)**: Raw cost ÷ Usable yield.

  - _Example_: €25 ÷ 0.8 = €31.25/kg.

- **Usage**: Price menu items with this corrected EP cost.

## Cooked Yield Test

- **Purpose**: Find shrink after cooking.

- **Calculation**: 0.8 kg raw → 0.6 kg cooked → 25 % shrink.

- **Cooked Cost**: EP cost ÷ Cooked yield.

  - _Example_: €31.25 ÷ 0.75 = €41.67/kg as served.

- **Record Keeping**: Record yields in a binder; update when supplier changes trim or cut.
