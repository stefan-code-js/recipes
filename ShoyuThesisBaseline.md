# Shoyu: Thesis & Baseline

**Thesis**: Brined grain/legume moromi yields complex, pressable seasoning liquid.

**Baseline Playbook**:

1. Make grain/legume koji.

2. Cover with cooled, strong brine to create the _moromi_ mash.

3. Ferment cool for **3–4 months**, stirring occasionally.

4. Press, strain, bottle, and refrigerate.

**Safety Rail**: Cool-room moromi fermentation for **3–4 months** before pressing and clarifying.
