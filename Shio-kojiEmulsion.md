# Shio-Koji Emulsion

## Ingredients

- 60 g shio koji

- 120 mL grapeseed oil

- 20 mL lemon juice

- 5 g Dijon mustard

## Process

1. Blend all ingredients until thick and glossy.

2. Store refrigerated 3 days max.

## Parameters

- **Storage**: 3 days max (refrigerated)

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: lactic-saline emulsion to dress raw vegetables or poached white asparagus.

## Creative Application

Use the glossy emulsion to dress raw, shaved vegetables or poached white asparagus, providing a creamy, savory, and lactic counterpoint.
