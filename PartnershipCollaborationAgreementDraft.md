## Terms & Conditions (Key Clauses)

1. **Scope of Work**: Both parties agree to the detailed scope of work attached as Schedule A. Any changes must be mutually agreed upon in writing.

2. **Intellectual Property**: All pre-existing IP remains the property of the originating party. Any new IP created during the collaboration (e.g., new recipes, branding) shall be jointly owned 50/50 unless otherwise specified.

3. **Confidentiality**: Both parties are bound by the terms of a mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement.

4. **Termination**: Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice in the event of a material breach of this agreement.

5. **Brand Use**: Each party grants the other a limited, non-exclusive license to use their respective brand assets solely for the purpose of this collaboration.
