# Roasting Techniques and Control Points

## Low-Temp Roasting

- **Temp**: 120–165 °C

- **Result**: Even doneness, high yield.

- **Example**: Beef striploin → sear pan 2 min each side, oven 140 °C to 56 °C core, rest 15 min.

## High-Temp Roasting

- **Temp**: 190–230 °C

- **Use**: Small tender cuts (duck breast, lamb rack).

## Resting

- **Purpose**: Lets juices redistribute.

- **Time**: 10–20 min for large roasts.
