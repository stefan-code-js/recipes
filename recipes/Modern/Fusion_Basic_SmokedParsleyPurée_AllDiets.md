## Ingredients

- Sixty g Parsley Purée
- Fifteen g Beef Garum*
- 500 g potatoes (small sized, from last year’s harvest)
- 400 g butter
- One Hundred mL Lacto Koji Water* (made with Øland Wheat Koji*)
