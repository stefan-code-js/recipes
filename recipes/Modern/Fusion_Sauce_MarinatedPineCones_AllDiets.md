## Method

1. reduced white wine to taste. Slowly heat the sauce and add the brown butter. The ratio should
2. be 3 parts sauce to 2 parts butter. Be sure not to boil the sauce once the butter is in; otherwise,the butter will emulsify, and the sauce will become too thick. Once the sauce is to temperature,
3. and just before serving, add the chopped truffles -approximately 2 g per person.
4. Heat the rice koji water until steaming and emulsify the butter in bit by bit using an immersionblender. Use a pinch of Xantana to help the emulsion bind. Use lemon juice if necessary to adjust the seasoning and water to adjust the consistency. For each pot of butter emulsion, addthe rose oil to season it and emulsify the oil in as well.
