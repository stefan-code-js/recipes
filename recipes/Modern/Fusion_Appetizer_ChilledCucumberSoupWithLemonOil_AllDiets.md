## Method

1. Put the cucumber pieces in the bowl of a food processor and process until pureed. Add the crème fraîche and pulse. Season to taste with salt. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours or until chilled.
2. To serve, divide the chilled soup among 6 small bowls or demitasse cups. Adjust the seasoning. Garnish each serving with a few drops of lemon oil and a sprinkling of chives.
3. zest of 10 lemons, removed in strips
4. Put the zest and the oil in a small saucepan over low heat and bring to a simmer. As soon as the mixture comes to a simmer, remove from the heat and allow to steep for 2 hours.
