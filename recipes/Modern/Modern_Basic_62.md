# Plant Garum (Yeast + Mushroom)

## Ingredients

- 1 kg fresh mushrooms, chopped

- 200 g nutritional yeast

- 120 g salt

- 200 mL water

## Process

1. Mix all ingredients in a container.

2. Incubate for 6 weeks (accelerated method).

3. Strain through fine cloth; bottle liquid.

## Parameters

- **Salt %**: 12%

- **Temperature**: 60 °C (accelerated method) or ambient (9 months)

- **Time**: 6 weeks (accelerated) or 9 months (ambient)

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: clear amber umami sauce; base for dressings or glazes.

## Creative Application

Use the clear garum liquid to season a vegetable consommé, providing deep, savory complexity without animal products.
