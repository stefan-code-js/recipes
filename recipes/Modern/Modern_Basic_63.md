# Poultry Cooking Techniques

## Temperature Ranges

- **Large Birds**: 120–165 °C

- **Small Birds**: 165–190 °C

- **Duck/Goose**: Tolerates 200–220 °C but dries fast.

## Doneness and Prep

- **Core Temp**: Always check thigh (thickest part) **≥ 74 °C**.

- **Skin**: Air-dry skin 24 h for crispness.
