## Method

1. mixture with the dried herbs and tea for 4 minutes, then strain.
2. Rinse the spinach and leaves thoroughly to remove all dirt. Pull
3. off the stems and discard. Steam the herbs for 1 minute in 4 table­
4. spoons of the tea emulsion, then add the spinach and another
5. spoonful of emulsion, steam for a further 20 seconds and season
6. to taste. Spread the mixture on a small sheet of baking (parchment)
7. paper inside round cutters of approximately 80mm diameter.
8. Peel the celery and remove any remaining fibrous strings. Cut
9. horizontally into small pieces.
10. Herbs and bread garnish
11. and strain. Keep on dry kitchen paper (paper towels). Pull the bread
12. into small pieces and saute them in a pan with butter. Remove when
13. crisp and season with salt.
14. Cut the cheese into small pieces and mix with the remaining
15. ingredients in a Thermomix for 5 minutes at 50°C (120°F).
16. Cook the celery pieces for 1 minute in the tea emulsion. Gently heat
17. the discs of spinach and herbs, place one on each plate and put the
18. celery, bread pieces and herbs on top. Foam up the warm sauce with
19. an immersion blender and add the foam to the plate.
