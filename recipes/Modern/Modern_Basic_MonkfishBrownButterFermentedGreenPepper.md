# Monkfish, Brown Butter, Fermented Green Pepper

## Prep & Cook

- **Prep**: Tail trimmed and tied.

- **Cook**: Roast to **54–56 °C core**; baste with brown butter.

## Sauce & Finish

- **Sauce**: Jus split with peppercorn oil.

## Parameters

- **Core Temp**: 54–56 °C

## Plating Cues

- **Texture**: Firm, succulent.
