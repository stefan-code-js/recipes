## Ingredients

- Three Plum Leather discs
- Quail egg trays
- Woodruff Ice Cream
- Eight g ants
- Two g reen coriander seeds
- Five g muscovado suar
- Five g Black Currant Wood Oil*
- 500 mL Gammel Dansk
- 200 g lucose syrup

---

## Method

1. Combine all the ingredients in a mortar and mix well using the pestle. Transfer to an airtight
2. container and reserve in the fridge.
3. Combine both ingredients together and mix well. Transfer to a pot and reduce on the stove byhalf. Remove from the heat, cool down, and reserve in piping bags in the fridge.
