4. Warm the broth up to 80°C. Once up to temperature, season the broth to taste with the abov e

6. Freeze the Douglas fir needles with liquid nitrogen and blend them in a Blendtec blender for 30seconds, until well powdered. Add the ethanol to the powdered needles and mix well. Run t he

8. Fill the base of the bowl with cleaned reindeer moss. Starting at 6 o’clock and working clockwise, place the sweetbread, then the tongue, then the mushroom, the bone marrow, the currants and finally the pine cone. Pour the mushroom broth into the bowl and spritz it with
9. Douglas fir distillate. Serve immediately.
