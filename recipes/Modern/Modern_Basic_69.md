# Quick Costing Example (Beef Tenderloin)

## Raw Cost

- Beef tenderloin raw = €25/kg.

- Trim loss = 20 %.

## EP Cost Calculation

- **EP Cost** = €25 ÷ 0.8 = **€31.25/kg**.

## Cooked Cost Calculation

- **Shrink** = 25 % after cooking.

- **Cooked Cost** = €31.25 ÷ 0.75 = **€41.67/kg**.

## Menu Price Calculation

- **Portion**: 180 g → €7.50 portion cost.

- **Target Food-Cost**: 30 %.

- **Menu Price** = 7.50 ÷ 0.30 = **€25**.
