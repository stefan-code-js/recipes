3. match the different varieties a nd colors of the leaves. Add 2 or 3 more branches of fir in
4. between the leaves. It’s important that the leaves aren’t too packed into the bowl. They need to
5. stand up, but not crush each other. Each plate will look different and unique. Keep the plates on
6. the hot pass -do not put them in the hot pass, or the leaves will dry and crack.
7. Add the cep oil and the Douglas fir oil to each bowl. Heat the seasoned deer- bushi broth to
8. 80°C. Pour the finished broth into skinny yogurt cups marked at 70 g. Proceed to pour these
9. broth portions into the leaf bowls which already have oil inside. Spray the leaf bowls with the Douglas fir water spray 10 times each. Serve immediately.
