## Method

1. This recipe uses a biga to make whole wheat bread. I like the way the earthiness of the
2. biga complements the flavors of the bran and wheat germ present in whole wheat flour.
3. And there’s the fiber benefit, too. This is an excellent sandwich bread. It’s great for toast
4. and croutons, and I like whole wheat bread with fresh cheese or with butter and honey. It
5. is excellent with liver mousses or pâtés, and maybe apricot preserves on the side. Toss
6. on some crushed pistachios if you have them. Boy howdy!
