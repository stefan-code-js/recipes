## Method

1. Salt-Crusted with Artichokes, Tomatoes, and Lemon Thyme Serves 8 CREPE BATTER 2 1/4 cups milk 1 egg 1 1/4 cups flour 1 1/2 teaspoons chopped thyme 1 1/2 teaspoons chopped rosemary 1 1/2 teaspoons chopped tarragon 1 1/2 teaspoons chopped chives 1 teaspoon salt In a large bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, and flour. Add the herbs and salt, whisking until smooth. Heat a large sauté pan over high heat for 2 minutes. Lower the heat to low and allow the pan to cool for 4 to 5 minutes. Coat the pan
