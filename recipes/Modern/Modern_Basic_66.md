# Quail, Fermented Honey Glaze, Thyme

## Prep & Cook

- **Prep**: Spatchcock; dry brine 1%.

- **Cook**: High-heat roast.

- **Glaze**: Brush fermented honey + thyme during last 3 min.

## Finish

- Lemon vinegar at pass.

## Parameters

- **Brine Salt**: 1%

- **Glaze Time**: Last 3 min of cook.

## Plating Cues

- **Finish**: Bright acid from lemon vinegar.
