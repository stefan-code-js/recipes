# Troubleshooting (Jus, Emulsions, Protein)

## Grey Fish Albumin

- Cook slower.

- Brine briefly.

- Baste.

## Tight Beef After Cook

- Under-rested; rest longer.

- Slice against grain.

## Split Beurre Blanc

- Re-emulsify with splash of hot water.

- Whisk in a cold cube of butter.

## Flabby Skin

- Air-dry longer.

- Start in cold oil.

- Press with spatula during sear.

## Dull Jus

- Reduce wider, skim more.

- Add tiny glucose.

- Finish with verjus.
