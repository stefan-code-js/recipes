# Salmon Mi-Cuit, Sorrel Sauce Verte

## Prep & Cook

- **Prep**: 1% salt 20 min; wipe (Quick Cure).

- **Cook**: **43 °C core** in oil bath OR **45 °C sous-vide 30–40 min**.

## Sauce & Finish

- **Sauce**: Blanched herbs + sorrel + fumet + lemon; blend with butter or oil.

- **Finish**: Pickled sorrel stems.

## Parameters

- **Cure Salt**: 1%

- **Core Temp**: 43 °C

- **SV Temp/Time**: 45 °C / 30–40 min

## Plating Cues

- **Texture**: Mi-cuit (just set).
