## Ingredients

## Method

1. Using a bit of liquid nitrogen, freeze the lemon thyme and the juniper skins in a mortar and grindthem to a powder with the pestle. Once ground, add the liquids to make a smooth paste. Once
2. smooth, transfer the paste to an appropriately sized container and fold in the arctic thyme. Reserve.
3. Blend the blueberries in a Thermomix until well puréed. F reeze the mixture in 1 L containers,
4. leaving room in the container for expansion in the freezer. Once frozen, remove the solid bricksfrom their containers and place them in cheesecloth in a shallow perforated gastro tray ove
5. eep gastro tray to catch the drippings. Keep this setup, covered, in the fridge for 2 to 3 days,
6. until completely thawed. Wring out the thawed mass for maximum extraction. Reduce the harvested blueberry juice in a dehydrator set to 60°C until it reaches a sugar content of 80 ºBx.
7. Reserve the reduction, vacuum sealed, in the freezer until needed.
