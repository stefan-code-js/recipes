## Ingredients

## Method

2. leave the insides medium -rare, spraying it with the Danish whisky. As it’s sautéing, torch the
3. meat quickly to barely flambé it and then pull it off the stove. Transfer the cooked leg meat to the pot of stew base. Add the maitake, sourdough, arctic thyme, chanterelles, brunoised duck heart, and dried lacto plums. Fold everything together and adjust the seasoning if necessary.
4. Light some charcoal in a barbecue to medium -high heat. Line a tamis with the rose petals, giving
5. each one individual space. Hold the tamis over the hot charcoal to lightly toast and grill the rosepetals. The roses will caramelize a bit but be careful not to burn them or dry them out. Once grilled, send the roses inside to finish the dish.
