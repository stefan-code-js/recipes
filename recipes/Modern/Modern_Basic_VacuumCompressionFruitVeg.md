# Vacuum Compression Fruit/Veg

## Technique

Apply 100% vacuum to fruit or vegetables with 3–5% syrup or brine → hold 2–8 h cold.

## Parameters

- **Vacuum**: 100%

- **Syrup/Brine %**: 3–5%

- **Hold Time**: 2–8 h

- **Temperature**: Cold

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: Intensified color and flavor, crisp texture.
