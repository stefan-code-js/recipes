# COLD -SMOKED KING CRAB

**Cuisine Type:** Fusion
**Course:** Salads
**Dietary:** AllDiets
**Season:** AllSeasons

**Prep Time:** None
**Cook Time:** None
**Total Time:** None
**Servings:** Four Servings
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

---

## Ingredients

- One king crab
- BERRY SPICE
- Five g coriander seeds, toasted
- Five g fennel seeds, toasted
- One g anelica, toasted
- Five g Dried Horseradish*
- Ten g Dried Giner*
- Two g arctic thyme
- Fifteen g Dried Strawberries*
- One g ants
- Fifteen g Black Currant Wood Oil*
- Thirty g Chicken Win Garum*

---

## Method

1. shrink when cooking.
2. Transfer the 6 large portions to a cold smoker speed rack, set up with smoking dust in a smokingcoil. Smoke the legs and halved body collars for 30 minutes (though if it needs more time, give it
3. more time). The shells should smell like almonds by the time they’re done. After they’ve beencold smoked , transfer the legs and collars to the fridge until service.
4. Toast the coriander, angelica, and fennel in a pan until aromatic. Once toasted, combine the spices with the rest of the ingredients and pulverize them in a spice grinder until finely
5. powdered. Reserve in an airtight container at room temperature.
6. Starting with the dry ingredients, grind all the ingredients together with a mortar and pestle.
7. Reserve.
