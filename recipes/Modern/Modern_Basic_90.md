# Training Drill: Plating & Texture Contrast

## Goal

Learn balance: crisp, creamy, acidic, aromatic.

## Practice

1. Pick a single ingredient (beet, carrot, pumpkin, cauliflower).

2. Build 3 versions:

- Purée or emulsion (smooth).

- Roasted or grilled (caramelized).

- Raw or pickled (fresh/acidic).

1. Plate them together; adjust proportion until flavor feels complete.

2. **Record**: Log ratios (e.g., 50% roast, 30% purée, 20% pickle) to engineer texture layers.
