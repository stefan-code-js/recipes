# CRÈME FRAÎCHE

**Cuisine Type:** Fusion
**Course:** Basics
**Dietary:** AllDiets
**Season:** AllSeasons

**Prep Time:** None
**Cook Time:** None
**Total Time:** None
**Servings:** Four Servings
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

---

## Ingredients

- Two cups heavy cream
- Two tablespoons buttermilk

---

## Method

1. Mix the cream and buttermilk together in a glass, ceramic, or other nonreactive bowl. Stir well, cover, and leave at room temperature for 10 to 12 hours or until clotted and the consistency of thick sour cream.
2. Refrigerate until needed. The crème fraîche will keep for about 1 week.
