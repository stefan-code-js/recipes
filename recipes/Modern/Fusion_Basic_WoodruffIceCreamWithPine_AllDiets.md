## Method

1. Working with the first 260 mL addition of milk, blend in the plum kernels with an immersion
2. blender, and allow the mixture to infuse overnight. The following day, working with the second260 mL addition of milk, blend it with the fresh woodruff in the Thermomix for a minute on highspeed. Then strain the mixture through a fine -mesh nylon sieve into a bowl over ice, pressing fo
3. aximum extraction. Strain the infusing plum kernel milk from the previous day through a fine -
4. mesh nylon sieve and mix it with the cream and all the dry ingredients, save for the Procrema.Heat the mixture on the stove to 60° to 70°C to melt the sugars and then pass it through a conical strainer into a bowl over ice to cool it down. Once cool, combine it with the reserved woodruff milk. Blend the whole thing in the Thermomix for 1 minute with the Procrema and then transfer the contents of the jug to a Pacojet container and freeze.
5. Place both ingredients in a bag and seal on maximum. Place the bag into a combi oven set to 68°C (100% fan) steam for 10 minutes. Once cooked, transfer the bag to an ice bath and cool itdown completely.
6. paste. Reserve.
