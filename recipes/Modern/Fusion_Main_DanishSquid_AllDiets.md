## Method

1. When fully dry, weigh the herbs and add the equivalent weight
2. of the grapeseed oil. Process at full speed at 60°C (140°F) in a
3. Thermomix for 12 minutes. Cool, then macerate for 24 hours.
4. Strain through a fine cloth, applying pressure for a few hours.
5. Mix the cream and milk and keep in the refrigerator until serving.
6. salt soft on the inside and produce juice when pressed. Remove the tops
7. and rinse the berries in water. Pass them through a vegetable juicer,
8. and strain the resulting liquid. Measure the sugar content with a
9. refractometer and adjust it to 17° with either water or icing sugar.
10. Pour into a metal container and freeze. When frozen, scrape the
11. granita to a powder with a fork and keep in the freezer until serving.
12. Herbs and garnish
13. Pick the herbs, put into ice water and dry them. Cut off the tops and
14. rinse the strawberries in water. Slice them thinly on a mandolin.
15. Fry the rye bread in a pan with butter until golden and crispy.
16. Put the plates in the refrigerator to get cold. Place a small square
17. of squid in the centre of each plate. Sprinkle with salt and the rye
18. bread crumble. Add the tarragon and dill and mix the verbena oil
19. with the cream and milk mixture, then add to the plate. Add the
20. green strawberries, then the granita and serve.
