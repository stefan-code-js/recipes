# Onion Bouillon

## Technique

Slow-roast onions (halved, cut-side down) at 180 °C to mahogany color → simmer with water (1:1 ratio), thyme, and bay for 60 min → clarify.

## Parameters

- **Roast Temp**: 180 °C

- **Simmer Ratio**: 1 part onion : 1 part water

- **Simmer Time**: 60 min
