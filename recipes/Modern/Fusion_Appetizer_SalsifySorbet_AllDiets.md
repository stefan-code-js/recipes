## Ingredients

- Three cups milk
- Ten black peppercorns
- One bay leaf

---

## Method

1. Drain the salsify and transfer to a large saucepan with the milk, sugar, peppercorns, and bay leaf. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Reduce the heat and simmer for 15 to 20 minutes or until the salsify is tender. Set aside to cool.
2. Remove the bay leaf. Transfer the contents of the saucepan to a blender and puree. You may have to do this in batches. Strain through a
3. or fine-mesh sieve into a large pitcher.
4. If using a conventional ice-cream machine, cover and refrigerate the mixture for at least 2 hours or until thoroughly chilled. Freeze in the ice-cream machine according to the manufacturer’s directions. Put the frozen sorbet in a chilled container, press plastic wrap against the surface, and cover. Freeze until ready to serve.
