1. Temper the eggs until room temperature. Set up a water bath at 68°C. Cook the egg in the bathfor 26 minutes, then remove them and cool them down in ice water immediately until completely cold. Crack open the eggs and rinse the egg yolk of any egg white under gently running cold water. Transfer the yolks to a container of the beef garum mixed with the shoyu.
2. Allow the eggs to cure, completely submerged, for 2 hours. Remove the yolks from the cure
