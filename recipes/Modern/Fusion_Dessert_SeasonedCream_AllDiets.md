# SEASONED CREAM

**Cuisine Type:** Fusion
**Course:** Desserts
**Dietary:** AllDiets
**Season:** AllSeasons

**Prep Time:** None
**Cook Time:** None
**Total Time:** None
**Servings:** Four Servings
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

---

## Ingredients

- SEASONED CREAM
- One Hundred g cream
- Ten g Lacto Koji Water* (made with Rice Koji*)
- Four or 5 Peeled Skagen Shrimp
- Five pieces Roasted Kelp Salt*
- Two Seasoned Rose Hip quarters
- Four g Bleak Roe

---

## Method

1. pipe the cream into the well, all the way around the other ingredients, add 10 drops of horseradish juice, and place in the 2 seasoned rose hip quarters. Serve immediately.

- Garnish

- Plating
