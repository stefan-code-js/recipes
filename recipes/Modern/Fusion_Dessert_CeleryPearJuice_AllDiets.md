# CELERY-PEAR JUICE

**Cuisine Type:** Fusion
**Course:** Desserts
**Dietary:** AllDiets
**Season:** AllSeasons

**Prep Time:** None
**Cook Time:** None
**Total Time:** None
**Servings:** Six Servings
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

---

## Ingredients

- One pound Bartlett pears, cored
- One small bunch fresh thyme
- One tablespoon fresh lemon juice
- Salt and freshly ground white pepper

---

## Method

1. in a juicer. Skim and remove any foam from the surface of the juice. Transfer the juice to a small saucepan and add the cinnamon and thyme and bring to a simmer over medium-high heat. As soon as the juice simmers, remove it from the heat and allow to steep for 10 minutes. Skim and remove any foam that rises to the surface.
2. Strain the pear juice through a paper coffee filter into a large pitcher and allow to come to room temperature.
3. or fine-mesh sieve into the pitcher with the pear juice. Add the lemon juice, stir to mix, cover, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or until chilled. Season to taste with salt and white pepper.
4. Stir before pouring into small, chilled glasses for serving.
