## Method

1. Heat the milk in a pan to 23°C (73°F) and add the remaining
2. ingredients. Pour into covered ovenproofplastic containers and
3. cook in the oven at 36°C (97°F) for 1 hour 25 minutes, or until set
4. with the same consistency as fresh tofu. Cool with the lids off.
5. Warm the mead with the honey in a pan an,d whisk in the butter.
6. Froth with a stick blender until a velvety texture is achieved.
7. Garnish
8. Pick the shoots, put into ice water and spin them dry. Keep them
9. cool on kitchen paper (paper towels) until serving. Break the bread
10. little oil for 2 - 3 minutes until golden and crisp.
11. sprinkle with salt. Transfer the cheese to a plate and cover it with
12. the rowan shoots and toasted pieces of bread. Warm the sauce
13. slightly, split it with the rapeseed oil and add to the plate.
