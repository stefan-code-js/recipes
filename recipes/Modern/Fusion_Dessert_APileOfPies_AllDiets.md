# A PILE OF PIES

**Cuisine Type:** Fusion
**Course:** Desserts
**Dietary:** AllDiets

**Prep Time:** None
**Cook Time:** None
**Total Time:** None
**Servings:** Four Servings
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

---

## Ingredients

- Ten bunches rhubarb
- 500 mL Clarified Rhubarb Juice
- Two g quince tea
- Three g dried roseroot
- Eight g sweet licorice syrup
- Twenty-Five g birch syrup
- Forty g suar
- Three gelatin
- Thirty g Rose Oil*
- One kg raspberries

---

## Method

1. Place a small portion of the clarified rhubarb juice into a saucepot, heat, and dissolve the sugarsand syrups. Once dissolved, return to the rest of the rhubarb juice to homogenize. In a large
2. mortar, add the dried ingredients, and smash with the pestle to aromatize. Muddle in the sweetened, clarified rhubarb juice. Transfer to a container and allow this mixture to infuse in the
3. fridge for 30 minutes. After 30 minutes, strain the juice, season with salt, and measure the Brix.
4. The juice should be 10.5 to 11 ÂºBx. If it is not, adjust accordingly with more sugar or more rhubarb juice.
5. Bloom the gelatin in ice water. Separate 500 mL of the infused rhubarb juice. Transfer 100 mL to
6. a saucepot, and heat with the bloomed gelatin until fully dissolved. Once dissolved, whisk backin with the rest of the infused juice. Transfer this juice to a container and allow the gel to rest
7. and set. This will take 1 to 2 h ours.
8. Once set, take 200 mL of this soft gel, add the 30 g of rose oil, and bring to the fermentation labto sonicate. Reserve in the fridge.
