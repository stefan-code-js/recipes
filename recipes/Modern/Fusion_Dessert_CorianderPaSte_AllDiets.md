# CORIANDER PA STE

**Cuisine Type:** Fusion
**Course:** Desserts
**Dietary:** AllDiets
**Season:** AllSeasons

**Prep Time:** None
**Cook Time:** None
**Total Time:** None
**Servings:** Four Servings
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

---

## Ingredients

- Twenty-Six g Yeast Broth Reduction*
- Fifty g coriander leaves
- One L filtered water
- Seven g lemon verbena
- Three g lemon thyme
- Seven g Dried Cucumber*
- Three g dried woodruff
- Ten g kelp
- Five g freeze -dried ooseberries
- Five g Doulas fir needles
- Thirty round red radishes
- 300 g Radish Cookin Liquid
- Butterfly Tart
- Vegetable Fudge
- 18 to 20 half -moon slices of Radish
- Fifteen drops Horseradish Oil*
- Two nasturtium flowers of different colors
- Four sage flowers of different colors

---

## Method

1. RADISH COOKING LIQUID
2. Bring the water to a boil and pour over the remaining ingredients. Allow the tea to steep for 15minutes, then strain the liquid and reserve.
3. g Radish Cooking Liquid
4. Bring a pot of water to a simmer. Seal the radishes in a sous vide bag with the liquid and cook for approximately 5 minutes. Once finished, shock them in ice water. Once cool, remove and reserve both the radishes and the cooking liquid. Slice the radishes in half and then into thin half- moons.
5. to 20 half -moon slices of Radish
