# Thermal-Blend Oils

## Technique

Blend herb/spice + oil for 1 min → heat to 65–80 °C for 10 min → chill overnight → fine-strain → vacuum-seal and freeze to hold.

## Parameters

- **Blend Time**: 1 min

- **Heat Temp**: 65–80 °C

- **Heat Time**: 10 min

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: Vibrant, aromatic infused oil with slow oxidation.
