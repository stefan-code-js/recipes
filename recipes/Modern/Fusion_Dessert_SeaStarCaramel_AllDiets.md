## Ingredients

- One kg ripe sea buckthorn berries
- One kg carrots
- 300 mL Sea Buckthorn Juice
- 700 mL Carrot Juice
- Forty-Five g Noma honey
- Twenty g pectin
- One kg frozen black currants
- Muscovado sugar
- 160 g Carrot and Sea Buckthorn Purée
- Six g Black Currant Purée

---
