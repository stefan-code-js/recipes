# Venison Loin, Juniper-Cacao, Blackberry Jus

## Prep & Cook

- **Prep**: 1% salt; juniper, black pepper, cacao nib crust.

- **Cook**: Hard sear; **52–54 °C core**.

- **Rest**: 8 min.

## Sauce & Finish

- **Sauce**: Blackberry + game stock; reduce to lick (Nappé).

## Parameters

- **Brine Salt**: 1%

- **Core Temp**: 52–54 °C

- **Rest Time**: 8 min

## Plating Cues

- **Flavor**: Iron-rich, bright acid.
