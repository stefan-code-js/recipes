# Toast the cardamom, juniper, and coriander together in a pan until aromatic. Combine in a spice

**Cuisine Type:** Fusion
**Course:** Desserts
**Dietary:** AllDiets
**Season:** AllSeasons

**Prep Time:** None
**Cook Time:** None
**Total Time:** None
**Servings:** Four Servings
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

---

## Ingredients

- One Moss -Breaded Sweetbread
- Grapeseed oil, for frying
- Black pepper, freshly ground with a mortar and pestle
- Kelp Paste and Egg Yolk Sauce from “Reindeer Heart Tartare”

---

## Method

1. grinder with the remaining ingredients and blend into a powder. Reserve the powdered mixture in an airtight container.
2. Preheat a rondeau of grapeseed oil to 175°C. Fry the sweetbreads until the exterior is crispy,
3. then remove from the oil, and drain on C -fold towels. Season the fried portion with Nordic
4. punch, salt, and freshly ground black pepper. Serve hot on a plate with a napkin immediately
5. sauce on the table. Encourage the guest to dip the reindeer sweetbreads and moss into the same sauce.
