## Method

1. With the motor running, slowly add the oil and blend until emulsified.
2. Transfer to a small glass or ceramic bowl and season to taste with salt and pepper. Use immediately or refrigerate the vinaigrette in a tightly lidded nonreactive container for up to 2 weeks. Whisk well before using.
