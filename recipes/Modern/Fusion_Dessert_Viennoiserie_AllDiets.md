## Method

1. Cooking time
2. Stand mixer+ dough
3. butter, diced, at room
4. of the stand mixer on low speed for 5 minutes until well combined. Increase
5. elastic. With the mixer running on low speed, add the butter and sugar and
6. knead until completely incorporated (6-8 minutes). Make sure the dough
7. temperature does not exceed 75°F (24°C). Place in a clean bowl, cover
8. with plastic wrap, and let ferment for l hour at room temperature. Fold the
9. dough once (see technique ), return it to the bowl, and cover with plastic
10. wrap. Chill in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes.
11. Divide the dough into 8 pieces weighing 2 oz. (60 g) each. Shape into balls
12. and let rest for 30 minutes in the refrigerator. Roll out each ball into a 4-in.
13. (10-cm) disk and place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Let
14. proof in a steam oven set to 75°F (24°C), or place on a rack in a turned-off
15. oven above a bowl of boiling water, for about 11/2 hours.
16. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C/Gas Mark 7). Press down firmly on the
17. dough disks with your fingertips to make 4 or 5 imprints on each. To make
18. dough disks. Place a small cube of butter in each imprint. Sprinkle turbinado
19. sugar all over the disks and bake for 8-10 minutes until golden. Immediately
20. transfer from the baking sheet to a rack and let cool at room temperature.
