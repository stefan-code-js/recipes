# Saffron Kombucha

## Ingredients

- 2.5 L water

- 300 g sugar

- 3.75 g saffron

- Starter kombucha (backslop)

- SCOBY

## Process

1. Steep saffron in sugar water at 60 °C for 1 h.

2. Backslop with starter kombucha and add SCOBY.

3. Ferment \~5 d to target pH.

4. Freeze to hold.

## Parameters

- **Temperature**: 60 °C (steep)

- **Time**: 1 h (steep), \~5 days (ferment)

- **pH**: ≈ 3.5 (target)

## Sensory Cues

- **Stop Cue**: pH ≈ 3.5.
