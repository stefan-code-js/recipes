# Roasted Vegetable Jus

## Technique

Roast vegetables until brown (190–220 °C) → pressure-cook vegetables + water (1:1.2 ratio) for 35–45 min → reduce to nappé consistency → season with salt and acid at the end.

## Parameters

- **Roast Temp**: 190–220 °C

- **Pressure Cook Ratio**: 1 part veg : 1.2 parts water

- **Pressure Cook Time**: 35–45 min

- **Finish**: Reduce to nappé; season with salt + acid.
