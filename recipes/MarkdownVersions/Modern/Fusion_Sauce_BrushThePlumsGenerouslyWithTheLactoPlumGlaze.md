## Method

1. Smoke the mushrooms in the cold smoker for 2 days. Once smoked, lightly roast the smoked maitake mushrooms in the brown butter in a sauté pan until they begin to crisp up. Once cooked, transfer them to a dehydrator to dry out until completely dry at 60°C. Once dried, chop
2. the mushrooms very fine and reserve.
