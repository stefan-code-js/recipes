12. Baked Clams Oreganata
13. chopped fine

16. 1. Open the clams (see Fig. 20.9 for technique). Catch the juice
17. 2. remove the clams from the shell. place them in a strainer over the bowl of juice. Let them drain 15 minutes in the refrigerator. Save the 30 best half-shells.
18. 3. Chop the clams into small pieces.
19. 4. heat the oil in a sauté pan. add the onion and garlic. Sauté about 1 minute, but do not brown.
20. 5. add half the clam juice and reduce by three-fourths over high heat.
21. 6. remove from the heat and add the lemon juice, crumbs, parsley, oregano, and white pepper. Mix gently to avoid making the crumbs pasty.
22. 7. taste and adjust seasonings if necessary. (Clams are usually very salty.)
23. 8. Cool the mixture. Mix in the chopped clams.
24. 9. Fill the 30 clamshells with the mixture. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese and (very lightly!) with paprika.
25. 10. place on a sheet pan and refrigerate until needed.
