## Method

1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan over low heat. Add the onions, carrots, and celery to the pan and cook, stirring occasionally, for about 5 minutes or until the vegetables are softened but not colored. Stir in the tomato paste, thyme, peppercorns, and bay leaves.
2. If using the wine, add it now and stir to incorporate the tomato paste. Add the bones and water. Bring to a boil over medium heat. Carefully skim off any fat and froth that float to the surface of the liquid. Reduce the heat to low and simmer gently for 2 to 3 hours or until somewhat reduced and flavorful.
