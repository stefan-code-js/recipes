# Dried Carrot Flower*

**Cuisine Type:** Fusion
**Course:** Desserts
**Dietary:** AllDiets
**Season:** AllSeasons

**Prep Time:** None
**Cook Time:** None
**Total Time:** None
**Servings:** Four Servings
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

---

## Ingredients

- White Currant And Elderflower Sauce
- Place of
- onion to encircle the entire flower.

---

## Method

1. Wrap the bowl in plastic wrap and place it in a 1 L container with a lid (this can be done in advance of service). During service, place the plastic wrapped bowls somewhere warm to temper and heat up.
2. When plating, remove the plastic wrap, and spray the drunken flower with the black currant wood water spray quite heavily (4 or 5 times), for sheen and taste. Season with salt. Then, in
3. andom manner over the top, place 3 unripe sea buckthorn halves, 2 small pine shoots, 1 piece
4. eeds, 3 clusters of the innards of skinned nasturtium pods, and 3 black currant buds. Over the
5. top, also place 2 dried bluebells, a sprinkling of carrot flower, and a sprinkling of arctic thyme.
6. Shake the warm white currant and elderflower sauce vigorously to emulsify, add 2 spoonfuls to
7. the bottom of the bowl, and serve.
