# REINDEER BONE MARROW

## Ingredients

- Four reindeer marrow bones
- One piece Reind eer Bone Marrow
- Butter
- One small pine skewer
- Thirty g Lacto Cep Water*
- Thirty g Yeast Broth Reduction* 5 Beef Garum*
- One small- medium fresh cep
- Douglas Fir Oil*

---

## Method

1. brine again and leave the marrow bones to soak for an additional 6 hours. Cut the marrow boneinto 1 to 1.2 cm thick slices and return the portions to fresh 1% brine to purge for 1 hour furthe
2. or longer, if they retain a lot of blood or color). Once completely purged, remove the marrow
3. bone slices from the brine, and compress them, in a vacuum bag, in the bone marrow marinade.
4. On the stove, heat a sauté pan over medium -high heat. Add a knob of butter and sear the bone
5. marrow bones. Once cooked, spear a small pine skewer into the marrow.
6. Combine all the ingredients and reserve.
7. Slice the cep mushroom in half, making sure it is bite size. Over medium heat, sear the cep in
8. grapeseed oil and a bit of butter until it begins to caramelize on its face. Glaze the mushroom in
9. on its seared side using the mushroom glaze. Season it with salt and dress it with 1 pine shoot and Douglas fir oil. Skewer the mushroom with the pine shoot on a cleaned, sharpened branch.
