## Ingredients

- Five g Roasted Kelp Salt*
- Two g reen coriander seeds
- Seven g Celery Juice Reduction*
- Seven g Fennel Reduction*
- Five g Lacto Plum and Tomato Reduction
- Thirty g butter
- One Hundred g maitake mushrooms, in palm -size clusters
- Thirty g Brown Butter*
- One loaf
- Grapeseed oil, for frying
- airtight container packed with C -fold towels to absorb any excess oil.
- Four wild mallard thighs

---

## Method

1. With a mortar and pestle, smash the kelp salt together with the coriander seeds until well muddled. Then add all the reductions, muddle well again, then transfer the mixture to a smallsaucepot, and whisk in the butter to emulsify over low heat. Once emulsified, keep the pot at room temperature until servi ce.
2. Smoke the mushrooms in the cold smoker for 2 days. Once smoked, lightly roast the smoked maitake mushrooms in the brown butter in a sauté pan until they begin to crisp up.
