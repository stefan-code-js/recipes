## Ingredients

- LARDO SPICE MIX
- One piece star anise
- Two g juniper berries
- Two g black peppercorns, toasted
- Five g coriander seeds, toasted
- One piece wild boar back fat
- Flake sea salt
- CANELLA SPICE BLEND
- Five g yellow mustard seeds, toasted
- Two g arctic thyme
- Eight g Lacto Plum Skin Powder*
- Eight g Noble Fir Salt*
- One kg parsley leaves
