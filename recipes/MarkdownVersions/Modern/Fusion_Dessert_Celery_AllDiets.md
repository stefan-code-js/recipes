4. Cut the celery in small pieces and blanch briefly in boiling salted
5. water. Blanch the peas and mangetout in the same way. Pick
6. the herbs and refresh them in ice water. Vacuum -pack all the
7. vegetables with the vegetable syrup for 12 minutes. Dry, and keep
8. Pick the mint leaves from their stems and blanch until tender.
9. Squeeze the leaves through a Superbag to lose all the water. Leave
10. at full speed with the oil in a Thermo mix for 12 minutes at 60°C
11. (140°F). Cool, and macerate for 24 hours. Strain through a fine
12. cloth, applying pressure for a few hours.
13. Put the plates in the freezer to get very cold. Place the yoghurt
14. and vegetables on top. Mix the whey with the mint oil and drizzle
15. a few spoonfuls around the parfait.
