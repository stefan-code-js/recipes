## Method

1. Roast the sunflower seeds with half the butter in a pan over medium -high heat. Add the
2. powders and dried spices while the seeds are still piping hot to aromatize them. Stir well, and
3. while still hot, continue to add the rest of the ingredients, then pull it off the heat. From there,
4. grind everything with a mortar and pestle until you form a smooth paste. Reserve in piping bags in the fridge until needed.
5. Blend all the ingredients in a spice grinder to a fine powder. Reserve.
6. In a pot on the stove, bring the oil up to 160°C. Once up to temperature, fry the leaves quickly
7. until they’re rendered translucent, clear, and crisp, and the bubbling has just stopped. Remove
