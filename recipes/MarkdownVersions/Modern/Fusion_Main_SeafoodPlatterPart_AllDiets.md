## Ingredients

- Five cucumbers
- One Hundred mL Clarified Cucumber Juice
- Two g coriander seeds
- Four g lemon thyme
- 150 mL Ice -Clarified Mirabelle Plum Juice*
- Twenty g unripe sea buckthorn berries
- Two g juniper berries
- Two g anelica buds
- Two g reen coriander seeds
- Twenty g pine shoots
- Five g Doulas fir needles
- 20 to 30 Venus clams

5. Pound the fresh ingredients together with a mortar and pestle, then muddle in a portion of thejuice, combine it with the rest of the liquid, allow it rest for 2 minutes, and strain it through a fine-mesh nylon sieve. Reserve the marinade in a cov ered container in the fridge until needed.
6. Clam Cooking Liquid
7. Fill a medium -large pan with a few centimeters of clam cooking liquid and cook 10 to 15 clams
8. over high heat. Make sure to use enough of the cooking liquid so that the clams can swim shallowly. As soon as the clams open, use a spoon to remove them from the pan and transfer them to a container with a bit of the chilled cooking liquid over ice. Repeat with the remaining
9. clams. Reserve any excess cooking liquid, chill it down, and strain it.
