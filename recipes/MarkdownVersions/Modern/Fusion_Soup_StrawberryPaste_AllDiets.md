**Course:** Soups
**Dietary:** AllDiets
**Season:** AllSeasons

**Prep Time:** None
**Cook Time:** None
**Total Time:** None
**Servings:** Four Servings
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

---

## Ingredients

- Five blueberries
- Black Currant Wood Oil*
- Morita Ch ile Oil*

---

## Method

1. the bottom of the bowl. Place 2 blueberries over the other fruits to build some height. Then, around the fruit, add approximately 25 g of the berry soup from a squeeze bottle. Garnish the soup with a good amount of the grilled lemon thyme. Season the soup with a bit of salt, and drizzle in 1 pipette of black currant wood oil and 8 drops of morita chile oil. Serve immediately.
