1. Mix the two ingredients and reserve.
2. COOKED WHITE ASPARAGUS
3. Wash and peel the white asparagus. Trim the asparagus down to a size of 8 to 10 cm. Vacuum seal the asparagus with the rose oil in a vacuum bag and cook in a water bath at 68Â°C for about
4. minutes, depending on the size of the asparagus. Once cooked, shock it in an ice bath and reserve under pressure in the vacuum bag in the fridge until needed.
5. Cooked White Asparagus
6. One hour before service, remove the white asparagus from the bag reserve the residual
7. cooking liquid. In a new bag, add 10 mL of the residual cooking liquid, 5 mL of the pickling liquid, and 0.5 g of the rose petal salt. Add the cooked asparagus to the bag and
8. compress it on maximum. Reserve this in the fridge until needed.
