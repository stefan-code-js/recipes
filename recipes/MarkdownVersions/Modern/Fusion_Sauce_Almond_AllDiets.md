ALMOND GEL Makes 4 cups 2 cups coarsely chopped bitter almond seeds 5 cups milk 2 tablespoons agar-agar (about 14 grams) 1 teaspoon salt Toast the almond seeds in a saucepan over medium heat until golden brown. Remove the pan from the heat, add the milk, return to the heat, and bring to a simmer. Remove from heat, cover with a tight-fitting lid, and steep for 1 hour. Strain and chill over ice.
