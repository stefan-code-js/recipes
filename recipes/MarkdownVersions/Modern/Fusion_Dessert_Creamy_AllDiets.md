## Method

1. WHEATGRASS
2. 10g whole einkorn wheat grains
3. 10g whole buckwheat
4. 1.25 kg pork ribs cut into small pieces
5. 40g wheatgrass
6. roast pork juice
7. Rinse all the ingredients in water except the Vasterbotten cheese.
8. Cook the oats for 10 minutes in lightly salted water. Cook the barley
9. and einkorn for 15 minutes. Cook the kamut, emmer and spelt for
10. minutes. Rinse them all in cold water until cool, and keep dry
11. until serving. Soak the buckwheat in plenty of cold water for 24
12. hours and strain off the excess liquid.
13. Bring the water to the boil and add the dried herbs. Leave to infuse
14. for 5 minutes, then strain. Discard the herbs. Mix the herb tea with
15. the millet and milk and cook in a pan for 8 -10 minutes, stirring,
16. until tender and creamy. Pass through a fine sieve (strainer).
17. Peel the truffles and blend with the chicken glace and stock.
18. Emulsify the oils in the smooth mixture as with a mayonnaise.
19. Season with vinegar and salt to taste. Spread the puree in a fine
20. 10g of puree per sheet. Freeze the puree, then cut out in rounds of
21. 8cm diameter. Keep_in the freezer until serving.
22. Roast pork juice
23. Preheat the oven to 230°C ( 450°F). Roast the ribs for 30 minutes
24. until very golden. Peel the vegetables and pan-roast them. Lower
25. the oven temperature to 80°C (180°F). Combine all the ingredients
26. in a deep gastro and add water to cover. Place a lid on the gastro and
27. cook in the oven for 10 hours. Strain the liquid and reduce by half.
28. Wheatgrass juice
29. Chop the wheatgrass roughly and blend it with the water. Put
30. through a fine sieve and season with a little of the roast pork juice
31. , Mix all the cooked grains with the millet porridge and the cheese,
32. and heat through. Add the pumpkin and flax seeds and season with
33. salt. Place in the middle of a plate and cover with a frozen truffle
34. disc. Heat the truffle disc with a blowtorch, warm the wheatgrass
35. juice and pour it around the dish.
