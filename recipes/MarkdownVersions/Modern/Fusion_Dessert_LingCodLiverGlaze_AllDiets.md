## Ingredients

- 150 g Mushroom -Kelp Broth*
- Fifty g Yeast Broth*
- SMOKED MAITAKE OIL
- 500 g maitake mushrooms
- 500 g Cep Oil*
- Fifty g Brown Butter*
- MORITA AND ANCHO CHILE OIL
- One Hundred g Morita Chile Oil*
- One Hundred g Ancho Chile Oil*
- Combine both oils and reserve.
- One bar Koji Cake
- Nordic -Koji Butter
- Three slices Confit Ling Cod Liver
- Egg Yolk Sauce
- Three drops Smoked Maitake Oil

## Method

1. Combine both ingredients and reduce the mixture by two -thirds to 66 g. Reserve. This should
2. also be used as the “Plate Glaze”.
3. Cold smoke the mushrooms for 6 hours on a rack with smoking dust until heavily aromatized.
4. Warm the brown butter to melt it and blend the mushrooms with the fats in the Thermomix on
5. high speed for 1 minute. Transfer the blended mixture to a pan and cook over low heat until itstops bubbling (approximately 1 hour). Strain the oil through a fine -mesh nylon sieve and
6. Combine both oils and reserve.
7. slices Confit Ling Cod Liver
8. Temper the ling liver portions for at least 1 hour before service and plate this dish on a hot passto ensure that the components are as hot as possible while plating.
9. Just before service, brush both sides of the bar of koji cake with melted Nordic -koji butter.
10. are evenly browned and caramelized. Send the grilled cake inside and slice it into 3 rectangles
