2. them at 60°C for 12 to 24 hours, rotating them every hour or so, to keep the juices in the center.Once dried but still chewy and slightly juicy in the center, remove them from the dehydrator,
3. and transfer them to a container to allow them to steam and cool down.
4. Bring the dashi and the herbs up to a simmer in a pot on the stove and place the cucumbers in
5. the dashi. Cook the cucumbers for 30 minutes at a bare simmer. After they’re simmered, take

8. Reserve the flesh for the paste. Cut the cucumber skin into 7 x 6 cm rectangles. Reserve the skins in an airtight container on parchment until needed.
