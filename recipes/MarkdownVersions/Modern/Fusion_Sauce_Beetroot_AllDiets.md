## Ingredients

- Four big, long beetroot (beet)
- Ten red gooseberries
- Thirty-Two dewberries
- red onion flowers
- 314 Beetroot
- Cook half the beetroot in salted water for approximately 1 hour
- Sauce
- Add a drop of vinegar and a sprinkle of salt to each half gooseberry.

---

## Method

1. Cook half the beetroot in salted water for approximately 1 hour
2. until tender, and skin them before they cool. Slice thinly on a
3. mandolin, then cut the slices into rounds of 30 mm diameter.
4. Peel the remaining beetroot and slice and cut them in the same
5. way as the cooked ones.
6. Top and tail ( trim) the gooseberries and cut them in half. Remove
7. any stems and leaves from the dewberries.
8. Mix the 2 juices and heat them to lukewarm.
9. Add a drop of vinegar and a sprinkle of salt to each half gooseberry.
10. Place all the berries in a bowl and arrange the finely sliced beetroot,
11. cooked and raw, around them. Crush the angelica and coriander
12. herbs on top of the beetroot. Add the rapeseed oil to the lukewarm
13. sauce, mix, add to the plate and serve.
