# SWEET WOODRUFF AND PLUM DESSERT

**Cuisine Type:** Fusion
**Course:** Desserts
**Dietary:** AllDiets
**Season:** AllSeasons

**Prep Time:** None
**Cook Time:** None
**Total Time:** None
**Servings:** Four Servings
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

---

## Ingredients

- WOODRUFF MILK
- 260 mL milk
- Seventy g fresh woodruff (washed; stems are okay)
- WOODRUFF ICE CREAM
- 200 mL cream (38% milkfat)
- Thirty-Three g dextrose
- Ten g honey
- Ten g milk powder
- Five g Trimoline
- Two g salt
- Forty-Two g Procrema

---

## Method

1. Blend the milk and the woodruff in the Thermomix on high for 1 minute. Strain through a fine -
2. mesh nylon sieve into a container over ice. Squeeze the woodruff through a cheesecloth for maximum extraction. Reserve in the fridge.
