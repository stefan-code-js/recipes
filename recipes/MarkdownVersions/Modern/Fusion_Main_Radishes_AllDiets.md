## Method

1. malt soil ingredients, then mix the 2 batches together by hand,
2. ensuring that no moist lumps are left in the mixture.
3. cream. Season the radishes with sea salt and insert them in the
4. cream. Sprinkle enough malt soil on top of the radishes to cover
5. the cream completely and the radishes partially.
