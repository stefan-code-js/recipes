## Method

1. Kneading Dough in a Stand Mixer
2. Stand mixer + dough hook
3. back of your hand. If it sticks, it means the flour
4. needs additional kneading.
5. Crumble in the yeast and add the salt. Knead on
6. is smooth and no longer sticky. Continue kneading
7. for an additional 5-8 minutes on high speed
8. to develop the gluten network. Place the water and flour in the bowl
9. of the stand mixer.
10. temperature should not exceed 75°F (24°C).
11. light can be seen through it (windowpane test).
12. If it does not tear, it is ready.
