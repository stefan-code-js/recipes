## Ingredients

- Four small lamb shanks
- Thirty small yellow beetroots (beets)
- One bunch elderflower blossom
- a little butter
- 312 Lamb
- Vacuum-pack the shanks with the chicken glace and ramsons
- Sauce
- beetroot juice. Season with salt and vinegar.
- Char-grill (charbroil) the lamb shanks and glaze in a few
- tablespoons of warm chicken glace. Place a portion of meat in
- the centre of each plate. Heat the cooked beetroot in a little butter

---

## Method

1. and cook them at 63°C (145°F) for 24 hours.
2. Divide the beetroot into 2 groups by size. Cook the smaller group
3. in water until tender and then peel. Peel the bigger group and slice
4. finely on a mandolin. Keep in ice water for 10 minutes to crisp
5. one-third. Pour all the juices from the bags containing the cooked
6. lamb shanks into a bowl and add a few tablespoons of the reduced
7. beetroot juice. Season with salt and vinegar.
8. Garnish
9. tablespoons of warm chicken glace. Place a portion of meat in
10. the centre of each plate. Heat the cooked beetroot in a little butter
11. and a few drops of cordial. Drop the raw beetroot into the rest of
12. the cordial for a few seconds to sweeten. Add the raw and cooked
13. beetroot to the plate, add the elderflowers and finally the sauce.
