## Method

1. In a small saucepan set over medium heat, combine the sugar and water and stir until the sugar is dissolved. Raise the heat and bring to a boil. As soon as the mixture comes to a boil, remove from the heat and allow to cool.
2. Transfer to a covered container and refrigerate for up to 1 month or until needed.
