## Method

Tomato Cloud with Basil Serves 8 TOMATO CLOUD 4 sheets gelatin 1 cup Tomato Water (see page 371) 1 tablespoon salt Bloom the gelatin by placing the sheets in a bowl of ice water for 10 minutes, until pliable. Warm 1/4 cup Tomato Water in a small pot over low heat and season with the salt. Squeeze out any excess moisture from the gelatin and stir into the warm Tomato Water. Once the gelatin has melted, add the remaining Tomato Water. Pour into the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the whisk attac
