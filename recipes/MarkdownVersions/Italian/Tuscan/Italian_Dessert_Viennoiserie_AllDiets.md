9. with plastic wrap or a clean dish towel. Let ferment for 30 minutes at room
10. temperature. Fold the dough gently (see technique ) and lay a piece of
11. plastic wrap over the top. Flatten to a thickness of 3/4 in. (2 cm) and freeze
12. for 30 minutes. Roll the dough into a 16-in. (40-cm) square, about 1/s in.

14. Using the chef's knife or pasta cutter, cut out strips measuring 23/4 in. (7 cm)
15. in width, then cut the strips into diamond shapes with 11/2-in. (4-cm) sides.
16. through the slits to open them up a bit. Place on a lightly floured baking sheet
17. and let proof for about 30 minutes at room temperature in a draft-free place.
18. COOKING THE FRITTERS
19. Heat the oil in the deep fryer to 350°F (180°C). Immerse the fritters in the
20. oil, turning them over once the first side is lightly golden. Drain on paper
21. towel and dust with confectioners' sugar.
