## Method

1. Bloom the gelatin in ice water. Heat the 60 g of lavender cream on the stove. Once up to
2. temperature, dissolve the gelatin into the cream. Take 2 spoonfuls of the crème fraîche and whisk it into the lavender cream to temper it, then pass it through a conic al strainer, into the
3. rest of the crème fraîche. Then fold in one -third of the Italian meringue until well incorporated,
4. then the rest. Then proceed to fold in the whipped cream. Mixing gently until well
5. homogeneous. Move it to the fridge to set for 10 to 15 minutes.
6. Once rested, take the red gooseberry and rose emulsion and fold it into the mousse in 2 parts.
7. Transfer the mixture to a piping bag and reserve in the fridge.
8. Mix all the ingredients together in a mixing bowl by hand until unified. Crumble the mixture between your hands onto a parchment -lined gastro tray and transfer it to the freezer. Once
9. frozen, transfer the crumbs directly to the oven and bake them at 160 °C (50% fan) for 30
10. minutes, mixing them every 10 minutes. Remove from the oven, let cool, and reserve in an airtight container at room temperature.
11. Blend all the ingredients except the aronia berry reduction in the Thermomix until homogenized. Once blended, remove the contents of the Thermomix jug and pass the paste through a conical strainer with ladle. After it is passed, add the aronia berry reduction at 6% by
12. weight to the glaze. Reserve this glaze in an airtight container in the fridge until needed. When
13. glazing a cake with this, it should be reheated to 60°C.
