## Method

1. paste in the bowl of a standing mixer
2. fitted with a paddle attachment and mix
3. on low speed until a ball of dough forms.
4. by hand or in the mixer, so that the dough
5. develops elasticity and silkiness. Cover
6. 2. Alternatively, you can let the dough
7. rest for up to 24 hours in the refrigerator.
