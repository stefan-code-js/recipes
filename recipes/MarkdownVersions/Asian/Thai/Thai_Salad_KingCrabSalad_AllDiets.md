## Ingredients

- SPICE MIX
- Ten g coriander seeds, toasted
- Ten g fennel seeds, toasted
- Five g yellow mustard seeds, toasted
- Five g arctic thyme
- Five g Dried Horseradish*
- SPICED KING CRAB BUTTER
- 150 g Smoked Butter*
- Thirteen g Roasted Kelp Salt*
- Fifteen g Peaso Water Reduction* 10 Kin Crab Garum*
- One g saffron
- One g sumac powder
- Two g Dried Giner*
- One g vinear powder
- 250 g parsley leaves
- Rapeseed oil, for frying

---
