## Method

1. Thermomix for 1 minute on full speed. Strain the liquid and
2. reserve. Cut the kohlrabi in long strips and vacuum -pack it
3. with the liquid for 10 minutes.
4. Pick the leaves from the stems and blanch the leaves tender.
5. Squeeze through a Superbag to remove the remaining water, keep
6. dry on kitchen paper (paper towels). When fully dry, weigh and add
7. the equivalent weight of the grapeseed oil. Process at full speed at
8. 60°C (140°F) in a Thermomix for 12 minutes. Cool, then macerate
9. for 24 hours. Strain through a fine cloth, applying pressure for a
10. Wash the spinach and blanch for 2 - 3 minutes until tender. Squeeze
11. out all the water and freeze in Paco containers. Process the puree in
12. the Pacojet twice and push through a cloth. Store in a squeezy bottle.
13. Slice the potatoes with a Japanese turning vegetable slicer and fry
14. in hot oil. Drain on kitchen paper before serving.
15. Put the plates in the refrigerator to get cold. Put a small square of
16. -squid in the centre of each plate and sprinkle with salt. Roll up the
17. bands of kohlrabi and arrange around the squid. Pipe small dots
18. of the spinach puree on to the plate and add the seashore herbs and
19. a few potato crisps. Mix a tablespoon of the pickling liquid with an
20. equal amount of parsley oil and drizzle this sauce on to the plate.
