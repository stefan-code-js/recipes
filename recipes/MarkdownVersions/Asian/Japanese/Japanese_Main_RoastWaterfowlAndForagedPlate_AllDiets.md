## Ingredients

- One Hundred g apple juice
- Twenty g Doulas Fir Oil*
- Ten g brine from Brined Japanese Quince*
- Four Danish grape leaves
- 185 g frozen summer truffles
- Fifty g Roasted Yeast Oil*
- One Hundred g Cep Oil*
- Fifty g Mushroom -Kelp Broth*
- Twenty-Five g Lacto Cep Water*
- Five g Celery Juice Reduction*
- 3 to 5 spinach leaves

---

## Method

1. GRAPE LEAF COOKING LIQUID
2. Juice the apple scraps left over from hollowing out apples for the “Apple” serving. Strain
3. through a fine -mesh nylon sieve. Combine the apple juice with the other liquids.
4. Grape Leaf Cooking Liquid
5. Bag the wine leaves in a vacuum bag with the cooking liquid, no more than 3 leaves thick. Blanch
6. the vacuumed leaves for 2 to 4 minutes in boiling water depending on the size and thickness ofthe leaves. Immediately shock in an ice bath. Keep the blanched leaves in a vacuum bag in the
7. g Roasted Yeast Oil*
8. Thermomix. Once it comes together, proceed to add the oils until the mixture emulsifies like a
9. mayonnaise. Do not pass it. Reserve the paste in a piping bag in the fridge until needed.
