## Ingredients

- MUSSEL AND TOMATO BROTH
- One Hundred g Ice -Clarified Tomato Water*
- Fifteen g Lacto Koji Water* (made with Rice Koji*)
- Thirty g Ice -Clarified Mussel Juice
- Five g unripe sea buckthorn berries
- Ten g frozen Japanese quince
- Five or 6 Tomatoes
- One pipette Elderflower Oil*
- Two spoons Sumac Granita
