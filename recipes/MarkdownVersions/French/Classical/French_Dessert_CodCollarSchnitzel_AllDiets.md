## Ingredients

- Dissolve the salt into the dashi and reserve in the fridge.
- One cod head
- 15 to 20 g in weiht left at the end of the bone. Imaine a lollipop, but with cod instead of candy. Submere the cleaned collar pieces in the dashi brine, and cold smoke in the brine
- ROSE SPICE MIX
- Two g dried rose petals
- One g Dried Giner*
- One g Dried Horseradish*
- Two g dried ceps
- Two morita chiles, seeded
- One g coriander seeds, toasted
- One g fennel seeds, toasted
- One g black peppercorns, toasted
- Two g Roasted Kelp Powder*
- 200 g rice flour 110 all-purpose flour

---

## Method

1. ROSE SPICE MIX
2. g Roasted Kelp Powder*
3. Combine the ingredients in a spice grinder and blend till combined. Reserve in an airtight container.
