## Method

1. Sorbet with Olive Oil and Black Pepper Serves 8 MALT FRENCH MERINGUE 2 egg whites 1/3 cup sugar 1 1/2 tablespoons malted milk powder Line an inverted baking sheet with acetate. Preheat the oven to 175Â°F. In the metal bowl of a stand mixer, whip the egg whites until very soft peaks form. Slowly add the sugar and continue to whip to stiff, glossy peaks. Fold in the malted milk powder, a little at a time. Thinly spread the meringue onto the acetate and dry overnight in the oven. Peel off the acetat
