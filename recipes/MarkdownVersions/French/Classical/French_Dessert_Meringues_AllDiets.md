## Method

1. Cooking time
2. Silicone baking matStand mixer fitted
3. tsp (5 ml) white vinegar1 pinch of saltScant 1 cup (6 oz./ 175 g)
4. Preheat the oven to 200°F-225°F (95°C -105°C /Gas mark ¼).
5. Line a baking sheet with the silicone baking mat and place it on another baking sheet.
6. Whisk the egg whites, vinegar, and salt in the stand mixer at medium-high speed until firm and frothy.
7. Whisk in the superfine sugar gradually so the whites do not deflate. 1 •
