## Method

1. Couscous Salad with Tomatoes, Cucumbers, and Melon Serves 8 GREEK LABNE 1 cup Greek yogurt Olive oil Line a colander with 10 layers of cheesecloth. Place the colander in a large bowl, making sure that there is room between the bottom of the colander and the bowl. Strain the yogurt through the cheesecloth for 2 days in the refrigerator. Roll the strained yogurt into small balls about 1/2 inch in diameter and store in the refrigerator in olive oil to cover. CHERRY TOMATO CONFIT 24 cherry tomatoes
