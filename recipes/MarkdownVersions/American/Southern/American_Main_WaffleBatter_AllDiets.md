## Method

1. Preheat the waffle iron in a combi oven at 250°C (100% fan) set to dry heat. Pull the pans out,
2. and double check the internal temperature of the waffle iron, on both plates, which should besitting at around 220°C. Transfer the iron to a BBQ raised slightly above the coals to avoid largeflareups. Spray both sides of the waffle iron with fat spray to coat them and pipe approximately150 g of waffle batter into the hot waffle iron. Close the iron, and flip it over immediately, setting a timer for 90 seconds. During that 90 -second cooking time, the waffle iron should be
3. flipped 2 or 3 times to ensure even cooking. Mix the cloudberry mousse ingredients together,spoon 70 g of the mousse into a ramekin, and top it with the candied cloudberries. Remove the
4. waffle from the pan once cooked, crispy, and well caramelized. Transfer the waffle to a napkin -
5. lined plate and serve immediately with the mousse.
