2. until the proteins set and separate from the liquid. The clarified liquid is
3. reduced to make a shrimp and koji reduction.
4. Melted butter is cooked with dried Skagen shrimp tails and dried
5. roses. The mixture is blended and the shrimp butter is strained and
6. reserved.
7. Chicken stock is reduced to a light glaze, then mixed with some of
