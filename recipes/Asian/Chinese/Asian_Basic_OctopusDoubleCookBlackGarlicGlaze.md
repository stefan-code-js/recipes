# Octopus, Double Cook, Black Garlic Glaze

## Prep & Cook

- **Prep**: Simmer **82–85 °C till tender 60–90 min**; chill.

- **Cook**: Grill hard.

## Glaze & Finish

- **Glaze**: Black garlic + soy + verjus.

## Parameters

- **Simmer Temp/Time**: 82–85 °C / 60–90 min

## Plating Cues

- **Texture**: Char + gelatin snap.
