# POTATO MAGMA

**Course:** Soups
**Dietary:** AllDiets

**Prep Time:** None
**Cook Time:** None
**Total Time:** None
**Servings:** Four Servings

## Ingredients

- 500 g new potatoes
- Butter
- POTATO STOCK
- Two L filtered water
- Twenty g fresh elderflower
- MAGMA BROTH
- One Hundred g Passed New Potatoes
- One potted plant (parsley or lemon thyme)
- One piece Japanese knotweed (cut to 12 cm), to use as a straw
- Four g Elderflower Oil*
- Sixty g Mama Broth
