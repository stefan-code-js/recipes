## Ingredients

- Four g yellow mustard seeds, toasted
- Four g coriander seeds, toasted
- One g fennel seeds, toasted
- One g saffron
- One g sumac powder
- Two g Giner Powder*
- One g vinear powder
- Lemon Thyme Oil* 1 bunch lem on thyme
- Eight g picked Grilled Lemon Thyme leaves
- Ten g Yeast Broth Reduction*
- Fifteen g Lemon Thyme Oil*
- Ten g Cucumber Juice Reduction*
- Twenty g Peaso Water Reduction* 35 butter, at room temperature
- Fifteen g Black Currant Wood Oil*

---

## Method

1. Dress the lemon thyme in the oil and grill it gently in a tamis over a medium -heat barbecue until
2. lightly charred and crisp. Once grilled, remove it from the barbecue and transfer it to a C -fold
3. towel -lined container. Pick the leaves, being mindful not to take any stem, and reserve them in
4. Muddle the grilled leaves with the yeast broth reduction and then drizzle in the oil to emulsify.Reserve in an airtight container in the fridge.
5. In a large bowl using a whisk, combine all the ingredients together thoroughly without aerating the mixture. Reserve in a piping bag in the fridge.
