## Ingredients

- Forty g Kaelder Øl laer
- Four g yellow mustard seeds, toas ted
- Four g coriander seeds, toasted
- One g fennel seeds, toasted
- One g saffron

## Method

1. come together. Add the butter and continue kneading under the dough comes together
2. evenly. Allow the dough to rest for 30 minutes in the fridge. Once rested, use a rolling pin to flatten out the ball and then run the dough through a pasta machine till you reach setting 1.75.
3. Roll the dough into a long sheet, dust it well with additional flour, and cut the flatbread into 9 x6 cm rectangles. Grill these cut pieces of flatbread on a medium -low barbecue lit with coals,
4. until they puff slightly, brown a bit, and become crispy. Once grilled, break some of the edges o
5. he flatbread to make it appear slightly rustic and not perfectly square. Reserve the flatbread in
6. a dry airtight container for service.
7. Toast the mustard, coriander, and fennel seeds together in a pan until deeply aromatic, then combine in a spice grinder with the other ingredients and blend to a fine powder.
