# Rose Kombucha

## Ingredients

- Rose sugar water

- Finished kombucha (for backslop)

- SCOBY

## Process

1. Make rose sugar water.

2. Add finished kombucha to lower pH.

3. Add SCOBY.

4. Ferment 7–10 d to target pH.

5. Freeze to hold.

## Parameters

- **Temperature**: Not specified (assume standard kombucha temp)

- **Time**: 7–10 days

- **pH**: ≈ 3.5 (target)

## Sensory Cues

- **Stop Cue**: pH ≈ 3.5.
