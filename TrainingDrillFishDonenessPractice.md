# Training Drill: Fish Doneness Practice

## Goal

Establish the restaurant’s “signature doneness” visually.

## Practice

1. Cook six salmon fillets to specific core temperatures:

- 42 °C

- 45 °C

- 48 °C

- 50 °C

- 52 °C

- 55 °C

1. Record flake separation, opacity, and albumin leakage for each.

2. Decide your restaurant’s “signature doneness” visually based on the results.
