# Mise en Place Checklists (Condensed)

## Stocks/Jus

- Label with date, Brix or reduction %, last salt/acid check.

## Oils

- Label with date, base oil, infusion temp, botanical, freeze-by.

## Pickles/Lacto

- Label with start pH/salt, target pH, stop date.

- Keep brine separate from solids for service.

## Crunch

- Count per cover + 10%; backups in dehydrator.

## Pass

- Finishing acids, microplanes, atomizers, hot spoons, warm plates.
