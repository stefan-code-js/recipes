# Training Drill: Seasoning + Acid Balance in Sauces

## Goal

Train final flavor tuning.

## Practice

1. Prepare one neutral jus.

2. Finish small samples with:

- Lemon juice

- Verjus

- Sherry vinegar

- Apple cider vinegar

- Kombucha reduction

1. Taste side-by-side; note how each acid type changes perception of salt, body, and richness.

2. **Action**: Decide “house acid” for each sauce base.
