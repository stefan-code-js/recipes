# Troubleshooting: Sauce Dull/Flat

## Problem

Sauce is dull or flat.

## Solution

1. Add a tiny amount of glucose for shine.

2. Finish with high-tone acid (e.g., verjus, champagne vinegar).

3. Check salt level again after adding acid.
