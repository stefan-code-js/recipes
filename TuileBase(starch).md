# Tuile Base (Starch)

## Technique

Combine 100 g juice + 14–16 g starch + 8–10 g oil + pinch salt → cook to translucent paste → thin-spread → bake 150–160 °C for 8–12 min → dry.

## Parameters

- **Juice:Starch:Oil Ratio**: 100 : 14–16 : 8–10

- **Bake Temp**: 150–160 °C

- **Bake Time**: 8–12 min

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: Thin, crisp tuile texture.
