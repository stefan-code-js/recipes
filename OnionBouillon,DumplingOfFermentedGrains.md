# Onion Bouillon, Dumpling of Fermented Grains

## Components

- Clarified onion bouillon

- Dumpling made from cooked grains + koji paste

- Chive oil

## Method

- Poach dumplings 3 min.

- Float in bouillon.

- Ring with chive oil.

## Parameters

- **Poach Time**: 3 min

## Plating Cues

- **Clarity**: Tea-like clarity of the bouillon.

- **Flavor**: Deep umami, herbal top-note.

## Creative Pairing

A refined, clear bouillon service where the deep, caramelized umami of the onion is contrasted by the bright, fresh herbal note of the chive oil.
