PROCEDURE for Steaming Vegetables
This method is used both for pressurized and nonpressurized 
compartment steam cookers and for simple rangetop steamers that consist of a perforated basket over a pot of boiling water.
1. Know your equipment. Read all operating instructions sup-plied with your equipment. Each model is a little different.
2. Collect all equipment and food products.
3. Trim and cut vegetables as required.
4. Preheat the steamer.
5. Arrange vegetables in pans or baskets for cooking. Make shallow, even layers for uniform cooking.
•	Use perforated pans for best steam circulation.
•	Use solid pans if cooking liquid must be retained.
