918 Chapter 31  bakeshop production: basic principles and ingredients
Chocolate and Cocoa
chocolate and cocoa are derived from cocoa or cacao beans. When the beans are roasted and 
ground, the resulting product is called chocolate liquor, which contains a white or yellowish fat 
called cocoa butter.
Cocoa  is the dry powder that remains after part of the cocoa butter is removed from 
chocolate liquor.

easily dissolved in liquids than regular cocoa.Bitter or unsweetened chocolate is straight chocolate liquor. in some less expensive brands,
