Pastry cream (see technique p.  196)Filling Éclairs
Using the pointed tip, pierce 3 holes in the base of the baked and 
cooled éclairs, one at each end and one in the center.  
This ensures the éclairs can be filled evenly with the pastry cream.
Whisk or beat the cream until smooth. Spoon it into the pastry 
bag and pipe it into the éclairs through the holes. As the éclairs are filled you will feel them becoming heavier. Chill in the refrigerator until you are ready to ice them 
(see technique p.  169).1 • 
2 • CHEFS’ NOTES
• When filling choux puffs, pierce holes according  
to their size and shape. For religieuses, for example, a single hole  
in the center of the base of both the “head” (smaller puff)  
and the “body” (larger puff) are all that’s needed.
