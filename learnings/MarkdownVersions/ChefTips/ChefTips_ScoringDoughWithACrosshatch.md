58 At the end of the proofing time, when the dough is ready to bake, dust it 
evenly with flour. Using the lame, make parallel diagonal cuts across the 
bread in one direction, then repeat in the opposite direction to obtain 
equal-sized diamonds. 
CHEFS' NOTES 
The deeper the cut, the Aatter the bread will be after baking. 
Shallower cuts will produce a more rounded loaf.
