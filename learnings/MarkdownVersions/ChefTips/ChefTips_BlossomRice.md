## The Insight

166 BANANA­
BLOSSOM RICE 
NAHM -PIM TECHAMUANVIVIT 
At the COMO Metropolitan Bangkok hotel's restaurant, Nahm, an entic­
ing package of banana-blossom rice is presented in a bowl formed by a banana leaf, 
a choice that plays with the idea of using food as a vessel for other ingredients. The 
leaf also encourages the diner to consider the origin of the dish, which was created 
by chef Pim Techamuanvivit. Grilling part of the leaf introduces textural contrast, 
while the color and placement of the magenta-hued leaves provide tonal and 
geometric variation that creates balance while both heightening and comple­
menting the colors of the rice. The blunt, purposefully askew lines inadvertently 
recall a Vidal Sassoon haircut: fresh, bold, and decisive.

## Detailed Explanation

166 BANANA­
BLOSSOM RICE 
NAHM -PIM TECHAMUANVIVIT 
At the COMO Metropolitan Bangkok hotel's restaurant, Nahm, an entic­
ing package of banana-blossom rice is presented in a bowl formed by a banana leaf, 
a choice that plays with the idea of using food as a vessel for other ingredients. The 
leaf also encourages the diner to consider the origin of the dish, which was created 
by chef Pim Techamuanvivit. Grilling part of the leaf introduces textural contrast, 
while the color and placement of the magenta-hued leaves provide tonal and 
geometric variation that creates balance while both heightening and comple­
menting the colors of the rice. The blunt, purposefully askew lines inadvertently 
recall a Vidal Sassoon haircut: fresh, bold, and decisive.
