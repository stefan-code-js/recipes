# Heat the granulated sugar and water in the preserving pan

**Category:** Chef Tips  
**Impact Level:** [High/Medium/Low]

---

## The Insight

555
Heat the granulated sugar and water in the preserving pan  
until the sugar dissolves and then cook to 250°F (120°C).  
Add the strawberries and skim off any foam.
Ladle the hot jam into the warmed jars, close them tightly,  
and turn upside down until completely cooled.2 •
