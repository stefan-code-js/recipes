# Meringue Croquante de Betterave #TabledAdrian #CulinaryArt #Cracker #Dehydrated

**Preface:** A vibrant, airy crisp, utilizing the natural pigment of beetroot powder to achieve a striking colour and delicate, melt-in-the-mouth texture.

**Ingredients**

- **Base (Yields 30–50):**

  - 150 g egg whites

  - 150 g granulated sugar

  - 110 g powdered sugar

  - 1.5 g beetroot powder (finely milled)

**Method**

1. Combine egg whites and granulated sugar in a bowl set over a _bain-marie_. Whisk continuously until the mixture reaches 54°C.

2. Transfer to a stand mixer. Whip on high speed, gradually incorporating the powdered sugar until stiff, glossy peaks are achieved.

3. Gently fold in the beetroot powder to ensure uniform colour distribution.

4. Spread the meringue mixture to a uniform thickness of 4mm on a silicone mat.

5. Dehydrate at 70°C for approximately 4 hours, or until completely dry and brittle.

**Technical Notes:** The precise temperature of the _bain-marie_ is critical for creating a stable, Italian-style meringue base.\
**Chef’s Intention:** A disciplined, textural element that introduces a deep, earthy sweetness and visual intensity.\
