# Pasta All'Uovo Classica #TabledAdrian #CulinaryArt #Dough #BaseRecipe

**Preface:** A foundational dough, relying on the purity of Italian 00 flour and the richness of fresh egg for a silken texture.• 3 eggs

**Ingredients**

- **Base:**

  - 210 g Italian '00' flour

  - 90 g fine semolina

  - 3 whole eggs (large, 60g each)

**Method**

1. Combine the '00' flour and semolina on a clean work surface. Form a well at the center.

2. Break the eggs into the well. Incorporate the dry ingredients gradually using a fork.

3. Knead the resulting mass for 10 minutes until the dough is homogenous and firm.

4. Wrap tightly and allow to rest at ambient temperature for a minimum of 30 minutes before sheeting.

**Technical Notes:** The 3:1 flour-to-semolina ratio ensures both elasticity and structural integrity during cooking.

**Chef’s Intention:** The integrity of the final dish begins with the disciplined preparation of this elemental base.
