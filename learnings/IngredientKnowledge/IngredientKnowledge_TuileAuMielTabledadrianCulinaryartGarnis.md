# Tuile au Miel #TabledAdrian #CulinaryArt #Garnish #Sweet

**Preface:** A delicate, crisp garnish, utilizing the natural sweetness of honey to achieve a fragile, golden structure.

**Ingredients**

- **Base:**

  - 30 g unsalted butter

  - 30 g liquid honey

  - 30 g all-purpose flour

  - 30 g egg white

**Method**

1. Gently melt the butter until clarified.

2. Combine the melted butter with the honey, flour, and egg white. Whisk until the batter is completely homogenous.

3. Spread the batter thinly and evenly onto a silicone baking mat or stencil.

4. Bake at 170°C until the tuile achieves a uniform golden-brown colour.

5. Remove from the oven and immediately shape over a rolling pin or desired mold while still warm.

**Technical Notes:** Precise temperature control is necessary to achieve the desired snap and colour without burning the honey.

**Chef’s Intention:** Provides a textural contrast and a disciplined sweetness to a plated dessert.
