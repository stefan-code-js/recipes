# Pasta All'Uovo Pigmentata #TabledAdrian #CulinaryArt #Dough #VegetablePowder

**Preface:** A classic egg dough elevated by the infusion of concentrated vegetable powder, providing depth of colour and subtle terroir.

**Ingredients**

- **Small Batch (1 serving):**

  - 70 g Italian '00' flour

  - 30 g fine semolina

  - 1 tsp vegetable powder (beetroot, spinach, or tomato)

  - 1 whole egg (60g)

- **Standard Batch (4 servings):**

  - 210 g Italian '00' flour

  - 90 g fine semolina

  - 3 tsp vegetable powder

  - 3 whole eggs (60g each)

**Method**

1. Sift the flours and the chosen vegetable powder together to ensure homogenous distribution of pigment.

2. Form a well and introduce the eggs. Incorporate and knead for 10 minutes until the dough achieves a uniform, saturated colour.

3. Rest the dough for 30 minutes before sheeting to allow the gluten structure to relax.
