# Seasonal Menu Concepts #MenuPlanning #Seasonal #LuxuryDining #TabledAdrian

> _Green = creative notes, bronze = client, ivory = strategy._

## 1. Printemps Riviera (Spring)

- **Theme:** Lightness, fresh herbs, early Mediterranean seafood.

- **Appetizer:** Asparagus and Burrata Tartlet with Lemon Zest and Basil Oil.

- **Main:** Line-Caught Sea Bass with Artichoke Barigoule and Saffron Emulsion.

- **Dessert:** Strawberry and Rhubarb Verrine with Elderflower Foam.

- **Plating Notes:** Use white space. Verticality for the main course.

***

## 2. Été Azur (Summer)

- **Theme:** Vibrant, sun-drenched flavors, raw preparations.

- **Appetizer:** Heirloom Tomato and Peach Carpaccio with Balsamic Reduction and Micro-Basil.

- **Main:** Grilled Lobster Tail with Smoked Paprika Butter and Sweet Corn Purée.

- **Dessert:** Lavender Panna Cotta with Honeycomb and Almond Tuile.

- **Plating Notes:** Bright colors, minimalist arrangement.

***

## 3. Automne Provençal (Autumn)

- **Theme:** Earthy, rich, slow-cooked, game.

- **Appetizer:** Foie Gras Terrine with Fig Jam and Toasted Brioche.

- **Main:** Slow-Braised Lamb Shoulder with Celeriac Purée and Gremolata.

- **Dessert:** Tarte Tatin with Crème Fraîche Ice Cream.

- **Plating Notes:** Deep, warm colors. Rustic elegance.

***

## 4. Hiver Intime (Winter)

- **Theme:** Comforting, complex, luxurious ingredients.

- **Appetizer:** Truffle and Potato Velouté with Crispy Pancetta.

- **Main:** Beef Tenderloin with Red Wine Reduction, Pommes Anna, and Glazed Carrots.

- **Dessert:** Dark Chocolate Fondant with Salted Caramel Center.

- **Plating Notes:** Clean lines, focus on texture contrast.

***

## 5. Végétal Méditerranée (Year-Round, Plant-Based)

- **Theme:** Celebrating regional vegetables and legumes.

- **Appetizer:** Roasted Beetroot and Goat Cheese (or Vegan Feta) Salad with Pistachio Dust.

- **Main:** Stuffed Zucchini Flowers with Ricotta and Lemon Zest, served with a light Tomato Coulis.

- **Dessert:** Citrus and Olive Oil Cake.

- **Plating Notes:** Natural, organic shapes.
