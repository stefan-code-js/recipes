## The Insight

CHILLED ASPARAGUS SOUP WITH CRÈME FRAÎCHE
serves 8
Ever since I tasted gazpacho, I have been a huge fan of all chilled soups, and none is better than one made with green or white asparagus. You can make this with the asparagus stems and save the tips for another use. Vegetable stock forms the base of the soup, which I garnish with pretty swirls of crème fraîche. The only other ingredients are orange juice and orange zest—tasty flavor accents with asparagus. I ladle the soup into demitasse or other small cups for a refreshing start to a meal.
