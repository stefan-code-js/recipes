Grate in the orange zest, whisk in the melted butter  
and the brandy or other liquor. Stir until smooth and then chill  
for at least 2 hours.Whisk in the remaining milk in several additions. 
Layer the crêpes on the plate as they cook.  
Cover with plastic wrap until you are ready to serve them.3 •  2 • 
6 • CHEFS’ NOTES
• The batter is best if made the day  
before you need it.• Before you start cooking, first season the pan.

This ensures the crêpes cook to a good color without drying out.
