## The Insight

CHEFS’ NOTES
• For a richer, softer pudding, stir in 2 egg yolks  
and a scant ½ cup ( 100  ml) of whipping cream  
when the rice is cooked.• If you prefer, bake the rice pudding  
in a 350°F  (180°C /Gas mark 4 ) oven for 20  minutes. 
• Accompany the rice pudding with homemade caramel sauce,  
a fruit coulis, or Chantilly cream.
