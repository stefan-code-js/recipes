## The Insight

Keep the pan over the heat. Combine the pectin  
with the superfine sugar and stir in, followed by the honey.  
Cook until the marmalade reaches 220°F (104°C).Make a syrup in the preserving pan with the granulated sugar 
and water. When the temperature reaches 300°F (150°C), 
add the chopped oranges. Skim regularly, removing the seeds that rise to the top.
Ladle the hot marmalade into the warmed jars, close them tightly,
