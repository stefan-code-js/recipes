EVERYDAY CAKES AND SIMPLE DESSERTS
EGGS AT ROOM TEMPERATURE
It can’t be said often enough: cakes and pastries cannot be made with eggs straight from the refrigerator! So, get into the routine of taking your eggs out of the refrigerator at least 2 hours before you need them. 
When making everyday cakes, eggs at room temperature can be beaten in perfectly, without firming up the butter or making the batter curdle. For mousses and other simple desserts that contain egg whites, the whites will whisk up more easily and the sugar will dissolve better.
WELL BEATEN
