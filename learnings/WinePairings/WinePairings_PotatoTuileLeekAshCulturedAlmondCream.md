# Potato Tuile, Leek Ash, Cultured Almond Cream

## Components

- Potato tuile

- Leek ash powder

- Cultured almond cream

- Chive tips

## Method

- Spread tuile paste thin; bake to lace.

- Dust with ash.

- Pipe cream.

## Parameters

- **Tuile Prep**: Starch-based (Tuile Base technique)

## Plating Cues

- **Texture**: Shattering crisp (tuile).

- **Contrast**: Lactic cream contrast.

## Creative Pairing

A textural study contrasting the shattering crispness of the potato tuile with the cool, lactic tang of the cultured almond cream and the earthy bitterness of leek ash.
