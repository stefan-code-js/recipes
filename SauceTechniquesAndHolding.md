# Sauce Techniques and Holding

## Mother Sauces

- Béchamel, Velouté, Espagnole/Brown, Tomato, Hollandaise.

## Modern Reduction

- Cook stock down until slightly syrupy.

- Season only at end.

## Finishing

- **Monter au Beurre**: Finish with cold butter for shine; serve immediately or it splits.

## Holding

- Hold sauces between **60–80 °C**, never boiling.
